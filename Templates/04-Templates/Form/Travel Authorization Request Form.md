### 1. Purpose
The purpose of the **Travel Authorization Request Form** is to ensure strict budget control and regulatory compliance prior to the commitment of Organization funds. This form serves as the mandatory mechanism to:
1.  Verify that travel is necessary to achieve the Organization's exempt purposes (Bylaws Article I, Section 1.3).
2.  Confirm that sufficient funds are available within the specific Grant or General Operating Budget.
3.  Ensure compliance with 2 CFR § 200.474 (Travel Costs) for federally funded activities.

### 2. Scope
**Applies to:** All Directors, Officers, Employees, Volunteers, and Contractors of Study GRC Inc. intending to incur travel expenses on behalf of the Organization.

**Requirement Threshold:** This form must be completed and approved **prior** to booking any travel that involves:
*   Overnight stays;
*   Airfare or train travel; or
*   Total estimated expenses exceeding $250.00.

**Exclusions:** Local mileage reimbursement for routine business within 50 miles of the individual's primary work location does not require pre-authorization via this form.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **GSA Rate** | The per diem rates established by the U.S. General Services Administration for lodging, meals, and incidental expenses (M&IE). |
| **Allocable Cost** | A cost that is chargeable to a specific grant or project because it is incurred specifically for that project. |
| **Lowest Logical Fare** | The lowest cost travel option (e.g., economy class) that meets the business schedule requirements without causing undue hardship. |
| **Tax Home** | The general area of the traveler's main place of business or employment, regardless of where the family home is maintained. |

### 4. Instructions for Completion

1.  **Submission Timeline:** This form must be submitted to the immediate supervisor and Finance Department at least **14 days** prior to the travel start date.
2.  **Budget Coding:** The "Funding Source" section is mandatory. Travel cannot be approved without a valid Grant ID or Department Code.
3.  **Estimates:** Travelers must use current GSA rates for Per Diem estimates. Airfare estimates must be based on economy class quotes.

### 7. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Traveler** | Research costs accurately; ensure travel dates do not conflict with other duties; submit form 14 days in advance. |
| **Supervisor** | Validate the business necessity of the trip and its alignment with the Organization's mission. |
| **Finance Manager** | **Critical Control Point:** Verify that funds exist in the specified budget line item. Ensure the trip does not violate grant restrictions (e.g., Fly America Act). |
| **Executive Director** | Provide final authorization for high-cost or international travel to ensure fiscal stewardship. |

### 8. Compliance and Enforcement
Failure to obtain prior authorization via this form may result in the **denial of reimbursement** for all expenses incurred.

Travelers who book non-refundable travel without a fully signed Authorization Form do so at their own financial risk. Falsification of estimated costs or business purpose constitutes fraud and will result in disciplinary action up to and including termination and legal action.
