### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this document is to establish a mandatory, auditable record of all physical currency handled during Study GRC Inc. events. This form serves as the primary source document for financial reconciliation and ensures compliance with **Bylaws Article VIII (Records, Transparency & Finance)** regarding the maintenance of correct and complete books of account. It is designed to mitigate the risk of theft, fraud, and error by enforcing **Dual Custody** protocols.

### 2. Scope
**Applies to:** All Study GRC Inc. volunteers, staff, Directors, and officers who handle physical cash, checks, or money orders at in-person events, fundraisers, or merchandise tables.

**Exclusions:** This form does not replace the automated reporting generated by digital point-of-sale systems (e.g., Stripe, Square) for credit card transactions, though digital totals may be recorded here for aggregate reconciliation.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Dual Custody** | The requirement that at least two (2) authorized individuals must be present during the counting of cash and the sealing of the deposit bag. |
| **Float (Start Cash)** | The fixed amount of small bills and coins provided at the start of an event to facilitate making change. |
| **Net Revenue** | The Total Cash on Hand at the end of the event minus the Float. |
| **Chain of Custody** | The chronological documentation or paper trail that records the sequence of custody, control, transfer, analysis, and disposition of physical assets. |

### 4. Instructions for Use

#### 4.1 Pre-Event (Opening Count)
1.  The **Treasurer** or designated **Event Lead** must issue a cash box containing the **Float**.
2.  Two (2) individuals must count the Float prior to the start of sales to verify the amount matches the "Opening Cash" recorded on this form.
3.  Both individuals must initial the **Section A** verification.

#### 4.2 Post-Event (Closing Count)
1.  Immediately upon the close of the event or shift, the cash box must be moved to a secure, private location (away from general attendees).
2.  **Two (2) unrelated individuals** (Counters) must sort and count the currency.
3.  Fill out **Section B** (Denomination Count) completely.
4.  Calculate **Section C** (Reconciliation).
5.  Both Counters must sign **Section D**.
6.  The cash and this completed form must be placed in a tamper-evident deposit bag.

#### 4.3 Discrepancies
If the physical cash does not match the expected revenue (e.g., inventory sold vs. cash received), the discrepancy must be noted in the "Notes/Discrepancies" field. **Do not** alter the physical count numbers to force a balance.

### 6. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Event Lead** | Ensures the Cash Count Sheet is printed and available at the event; ensures a secure location is available for counting. |
| **Cash Handlers (Counters)** | Perform the physical count under Dual Custody; accurately complete the form; sign to certify integrity. |
| **Treasurer / CFO** | Verifies the form against the bank deposit receipt; retains the form in financial records for seven (7) years per the **Records Retention Schedule**. |

### 7. Compliance and Enforcement
Failure to utilize this form or adhere to the **Dual Custody** requirement when handling organizational funds constitutes a violation of the **Internal Controls Framework**. Violations may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of volunteer status or employment, and potential legal action for theft or embezzlement.
