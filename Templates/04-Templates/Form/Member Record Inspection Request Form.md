### 1. Purpose
This document facilitates the statutory right of Voting Members to inspect the books and records of Study GRC Inc. ("the Organization") in accordance with **Texas Business Organizations Code (TBOC) § 22.351** and **Article II, Section 2.6** of the Organization's Bylaws. It ensures that such requests are made for a **Proper Purpose** and that the privacy of the community and the integrity of the Organization's data are protected against misuse, commercial solicitation, or harassment.

### 2. Scope
*   **Applies to:** All **Voting Members** (Supporting Members and Honorary Members) in good standing who have been members for at least six (6) months immediately preceding the request, or who hold at least five percent (5%) of the total voting power.
*   **Records Included:** Books and records of account, minutes of proceedings, and the membership list.
*   **Exclusions:** This form does not grant access to attorney-client privileged communications, confidential personnel records, or sensitive security configurations (e.g., CISO infrastructure details) unless explicitly authorized by law.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Proper Purpose** | A purpose reasonably related to the member's interest as a member of the Organization (e.g., communicating with other members regarding an upcoming vote). It explicitly **excludes** using records for advertising, sales, or commercial solicitation. |
| **Voting Member** | A Supporting Member or Honorary Member as defined in **Bylaws Article II, Section 2.1**. |
| **Inspection** | The review of records via secure electronic means (screenshare) or controlled digital copies, as determined by the Organization's security protocols. |

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Requesting Member** | Completes the form truthfully, defines a proper purpose, and pays any applicable reproduction fees. |
| **Corporate Secretary** | Reviews the request for legal compliance (tenure and proper purpose), approves or denies the request within 5 business days, and schedules the inspection. |
| **CISO / IT Dept** | Prepares the data for secure viewing (e.g., watermarking documents, setting up secure viewing rooms) to prevent unauthorized dissemination. |
| **Treasurer** | Calculates reasonable costs for labor and materials if copies are requested. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
*   **Denial of Request:** The Organization reserves the right to deny requests that are vague, overly broad, harassing, or lack a Proper Purpose as defined by Texas law.
*   **Misuse of Data:** Any member found to have used inspected records for commercial purposes or unauthorized solicitation will face immediate **Expulsion** proceedings and may be referred to legal counsel for violation of the **Texas Business Organizations Code** and breach of the **Non-Disclosure Agreement**.
