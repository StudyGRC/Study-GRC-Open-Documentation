### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this form is to provide a standardized, secure, and confidential mechanism for Study GRC Inc. (the "Organization") stakeholders to report suspected illegal, unethical, or violative conduct. This form ensures that the Organization captures sufficient detail to initiate a prompt and thorough investigation in compliance with the **Whistleblower Protection Policy**, the **Sarbanes-Oxley Act**, and the **Texas Whistleblower Act**.

### 2. Scope
**Applies to:** All Directors, Officers, Employees, Volunteers, Contractors, Members, and Community Participants of Study GRC Inc.

**Usage:** This form must be used to report:
*   Financial improprieties (e.g., fraud, embezzlement, tax evasion).
*   Governance violations (e.g., Bylaws violations, conflicts of interest).
*   Youth protection and safeguarding concerns.
*   Data privacy and security breaches.
*   Retaliation against individuals who have reported concerns.

**Exclusions:** This form should not be used for general employment grievances (e.g., personality conflicts) unless they involve discrimination, harassment, or illegal acts.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Whistleblower** | An individual who discloses information regarding acts of wrongdoing, misconduct, or corruption within the Organization. |
| **Good Faith** | The individual reporting has a reasonable belief that the information provided is true and discloses it without malice or consideration of personal benefit. |
| **Retaliation** | Any adverse action (termination, demotion, harassment, exclusion) taken against a Whistleblower in response to a report. |
| **Subject** | The individual(s) alleged to have committed the violation. |

### 4. Form Instructions and Content

**Instructions:**
1.  Complete this form to the best of your ability.
2.  You may choose to remain **Anonymous**. However, providing contact information allows the investigator to ask follow-up questions, which often strengthens the investigation.
3.  Submit this form via the designated secure channel (e.g., Secure Portal, email to Audit Chair, or sealed envelope).
4.  **Do not** attempt to investigate the matter yourself or obtain evidence illegally.

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Reporter (Whistleblower)** | Submit factual information in good faith; maintain confidentiality regarding the submission to protect the integrity of the investigation. |
| **Audit & Risk Committee Chair** | Receive intake forms; determine initial credibility; assign an impartial investigator; ensure non-retaliation protections are active. |
| **Investigator** | Conduct fact-finding; interview witnesses; review evidence; document findings; maintain strict confidentiality. |
| **Board of Directors** | Review final investigation reports; determine appropriate corrective or disciplinary action based on findings. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement

**Non-Retaliation:** Study GRC Inc. strictly prohibits retaliation against any individual who reports a violation in good faith. Any Director, Officer, or volunteer found to have retaliated against a Whistleblower will be subject to immediate disciplinary action, up to and including removal from the Board or termination of membership/employment.

**Bad Faith Reporting:** Individuals who knowingly file false or malicious reports with the intent to harm another person’s reputation will not be protected by the Whistleblower Policy and may be subject to disciplinary action, including expulsion from the Organization.
