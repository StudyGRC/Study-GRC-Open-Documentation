### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this document is to provide a standardized template and usage standard for issuing a **Final Written Warning**. This form serves as the critical documentation step in the **Progressive Discipline** process, formally notifying an employee or volunteer that their performance or conduct has failed to meet the standards of Study GRC Inc., and that failure to correct the issue immediately will result in termination of employment or service.

This document mitigates legal risk by ensuring:
1.  Clear communication of expectations and consequences.
2.  Documentation of due process and prior coaching.
3.  Preservation of the "At-Will" employment relationship under Texas law.

### 2. Scope
*   **Applies to:** All paid employees and long-term volunteers of Study GRC Inc.
*   **Exclusions:** This form is not required for cases of **Gross Misconduct** (e.g., theft, violence, harassment, severe security violations) where immediate termination is warranted without prior warning, consistent with the *Employee Handbook* and *Bylaws Article III (Removal for Cause)*.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Progressive Discipline** | A disciplinary model that assists employees in correcting performance or behavioral problems, typically following a sequence: Verbal Warning → Written Warning → Final Written Warning → Termination. |
| **At-Will Employment** | The legal doctrine in Texas stating that employment may be terminated by the employer or employee at any time, for any reason (that is not illegal), or for no reason at all. |
| **PIP** | Performance Improvement Plan; a document often attached to warnings outlining specific goals and timelines. |

### 4. Usage Guidelines and Template

#### 4.1 Prerequisites for Issuance
Before issuing a Final Written Warning, the Manager must verify:
1.  Previous disciplinary actions (Verbal or First Written Warnings) have been documented regarding this specific issue or a related pattern of behavior.
2.  *Exception:* The incident is severe enough to warrant skipping earlier steps but does not qualify for immediate termination.
3.  The content has been reviewed by the Executive Director or HR designee to ensure consistency and legal compliance.

#### 4.2 Delivery Protocol
1.  **Meeting:** The warning must be delivered in a private meeting (in-person or via secure video conference for remote staff).
2.  **Witness:** A third party (HR or another Manager) should be present.
3.  **Signature:** The employee must be asked to sign. If they refuse, the Manager must note "Refused to Sign" and date it.
4.  **Filing:** The executed document must be saved to the employee’s secure Personnel File (`HR-REC-###`).

#### 4.3 Form Template
*The following text constitutes the official template for the Final Written Warning.*

***

**CONFIDENTIAL MEMORANDUM**

**TO:** [Employee Name]
**FROM:** [Manager Name]
**DATE:** [Date]
**SUBJECT:** Final Written Warning

**I. INCIDENT / PERFORMANCE ISSUE**
**Date of Incident(s):** [Date(s)]
**Type of Violation:** [ ] Performance  [ ] Conduct  [ ] Attendance  [ ] Policy Violation

**Description of Infraction:**
[Provide a specific, factual description of the incident or performance deficiency. Avoid opinions. Cite specific rules, policies, or job description elements that were violated. Example: "On [Date], you failed to submit the grant compliance report required by 2 CFR 200, despite two previous reminders. This resulted in a delay of funding drawdown."]

**II. PREVIOUS DISCIPLINARY ACTION**
This Final Written Warning follows previous coaching and disciplinary actions regarding this or related issues:
*   **Verbal Warning:** Issued on [Date] regarding [Topic].
*   **Written Warning:** Issued on [Date] regarding [Topic].

**III. REQUIRED IMMEDIATE CORRECTION**
To maintain employment with Study GRC Inc., you must meet the following expectations immediately and on a sustained basis:
1.  [Specific Action Item 1]
2.  [Specific Action Item 2]
3.  [Specific Action Item 3]

*[Optional: See attached Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) for detailed metrics.]*

**IV. CONSEQUENCES**
This is a **Final Written Warning**. Failure to demonstrate immediate and sustained improvement, or any further violation of Study GRC Inc. policies, standards, or rules, will result in immediate termination of employment.

**V. AT-WILL STATEMENT**
Nothing in this document is intended to alter the at-will nature of your employment. Study GRC Inc. reserves the right to terminate employment at any time, with or without cause or notice.

**VI. ACKNOWLEDGMENT**
By signing below, I acknowledge that I have received a copy of this Final Written Warning and that it has been discussed with me. I understand the corrective actions required and the consequences of failure to improve. My signature does not necessarily indicate agreement with the findings, but acknowledges receipt of this notice.

**Manager Signature:** ___________________________________________________  **Date:** ______________

**Witness Signature:** ___________________________________________________  **Date:** ______________

*(If Employee refuses to sign, Witness shall sign here confirming delivery: __________________________)*

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Manager** | Drafts the warning using factual data; consults with HR; delivers the warning to the employee; monitors subsequent performance. |
| **Executive Director / HR** | Reviews the warning for legal compliance, consistency with *HR-POL-048 Progressive Discipline Policy*, and alignment with Texas labor laws. |
| **Employee** | Acknowledges receipt of the warning; adheres to the corrective action plan. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
This document is a formal record of the organization's attempt to correct performance.
*   **Failure to Improve:** If the employee fails to meet the standards outlined in this warning, the organization will proceed to **Termination of Employment**.
*   **Retaliation Prohibited:** Study GRC Inc. strictly prohibits retaliation against any individual for participating in the disciplinary process.
*   **Record Retention:** This document shall be retained in the Employee Personnel File for a minimum of 5 years post-separation, in accordance with the *Document Retention and Destruction Policy*.
