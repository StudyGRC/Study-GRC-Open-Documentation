### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this document is to establish the official intake mechanism for **Supporting Members** of Study GRC Inc. This form serves as the primary due diligence tool to verify that applicants meet the qualification criteria defined in the Bylaws, acknowledge their ethical obligations, and formally request voting rights within the Organization. This process ensures compliance with Texas Business Organizations Code (TBOC) record-keeping requirements.

### 2. Scope
This form applies to all individuals seeking **Supporting Member** status (Voting Membership).
*   **Inclusions:** New applicants and existing Community Participants seeking elevation to voting status.
*   **Exclusions:** This form does not apply to "Community Participants" (Non-Voting) or "Honorary Members" (appointed by the Board).

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Supporting Member** | A class of voting membership defined in Bylaws Article II, Section 2.1, reserved for individuals who have demonstrated commitment through financial contribution or volunteer service. |
| **Good Standing** | The status of a member who is current on dues (if applicable), has met volunteer hour requirements, and is not under disciplinary review. |
| **Due Diligence** | The investigative process used to verify the accuracy of an applicant's qualifications and ensure they pose no risk to the Organization's reputation or assets. |
| **TBOC** | Texas Business Organizations Code. |

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Applicant** | Provide truthful, accurate, and complete information. Update the Secretary immediately if contact information changes. |
| **Secretary** | Review applications for completeness. Verify eligibility (dues or hours) with the Treasurer or Volunteer Coordinator. Maintain the official Membership Roll. |
| **Treasurer** | Verify receipt of financial contributions for Option A applicants. |
| **Board of Directors** | Retains final authority to approve or deny membership applications based on the best interests of the Organization. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
*   **False Information:** Submission of false or misleading information on this application constitutes "Cause" for rejection or subsequent expulsion under Bylaws Article II.
*   **Privacy:** Applicant data is confidential and may only be accessed by the Board and authorized staff. It is not sold or shared with third parties, except as required by law (e.g., member inspection rights under TBOC § 22.351).
*   **Non-Discrimination:** Membership is granted without regard to race, color, religion, gender, national origin, disability, or age, provided the applicant meets the objective qualification criteria.
