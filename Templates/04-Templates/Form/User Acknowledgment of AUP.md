### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this acknowledgment form is to ensure that every individual granted access to Study GRC Inc. (the "Organization") Information Systems has received, read, understood, and agreed to comply with the **Acceptable Use Policy (IS-POL-007)**. This document establishes a binding agreement between the User and the Organization, ensuring enforceability of security standards and clarifying the consequences of non-compliance.

### 2. Scope
This acknowledgment is mandatory for all individuals ("Users") requesting or currently holding access to the Organization’s data, networks, or technology resources.
*   **Applies to:** Employees, Board of Directors, Volunteers, Contractors, Interns, and Third-Party Vendors with system access.
*   **Exclusions:** Public users accessing the external website only.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **AUP** | The Acceptable Use Policy (IS-POL-007), which governs the secure and ethical use of Organization resources. |
| **Information Systems** | All hardware, software, networks, cloud platforms (e.g., Google Workspace, Slack, GitHub), and data owned or leased by the Organization. |
| **User** | Any individual authorized to access the Organization's Information Systems. |
| **Work Product** | As defined in Bylaws Article IX, Section 9.3, any content, code, or materials created by the User during their service. |

### 4. Acknowledgment and Agreement Statements

By signing below, I, the undersigned User, explicitly acknowledge and agree to the following terms:

**4.1 Receipt and Understanding**
I confirm that I have received access to the **Acceptable Use Policy (IS-POL-007)**. I have read the policy in its entirety and understand my responsibilities as a User of Study GRC Inc. Information Systems.

**4.2 Agreement to Comply**
I agree to abide by all standards, procedures, and restrictions outlined in the AUP. I understand that access to the Organization’s systems is a privilege, not a right, and may be revoked at any time.

**4.3 No Expectation of Privacy**
I understand and consent that Study GRC Inc. reserves the right to monitor, access, review, and record all use of its Information Systems, including email, internet usage, and file transfers, to ensure compliance with the law and organizational policies. I acknowledge that I have **no reasonable expectation of privacy** when using these resources.

**4.4 Protection of Minors (Youth Safeguarding)**
I understand that the Organization serves youth populations. I strictly agree not to use Organization resources to view, store, or distribute pornography, sexually explicit material, or any content that exploits or endangers minors. I acknowledge that violations of this nature will be immediately reported to law enforcement.

**4.5 Intellectual Property Assignment**
Pursuant to **Article IX, Section 9.3 of the Bylaws**, I acknowledge that all Work Product created by me using the Organization’s resources or during the performance of my duties is considered "work made for hire." I hereby assign all rights, title, and interest in such Work Product to Study GRC Inc.

**4.6 Security and Account Responsibility**
I agree to safeguard my credentials (usernames, passwords, MFA tokens) and will not share them with anyone. I accept responsibility for all actions taken under my account. I agree to report any suspected security breaches or lost devices to the CISO immediately.

**4.7 Remote Work Security**
As the Organization operates as a remote-first entity, I agree to maintain a secure physical environment when accessing Organization data, ensuring that screens are not visible to unauthorized persons and that devices are not left unlocked and unattended in public spaces.

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **User (Signer)** | Read the AUP, sign this acknowledgment, and adhere to all terms continuously. |
| **Manager / Supervisor** | Ensure the User has signed this form *before* requesting system access provisioning. |
| **IT / Security Team** | Verify a signed acknowledgment exists before granting credentials. |
| **HR / Operations** | Retain the signed form in the User’s personnel or volunteer file for the duration of service plus the retention period (7 years). |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
Failure to sign this acknowledgment will result in the denial or revocation of access to Study GRC Inc. systems.

**Violation of the AUP:**
I understand that any violation of the Acceptable Use Policy or the terms of this acknowledgment may result in disciplinary action, up to and including:
1.  Immediate termination of employment, volunteer service, or contract.
2.  Civil legal action for damages.
3.  Referral to law enforcement for criminal prosecution (where applicable).
