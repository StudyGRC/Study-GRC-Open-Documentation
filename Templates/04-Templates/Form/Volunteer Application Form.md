### 1. Purpose
The purpose of the **Volunteer Application Form** is to standardize the intake process for prospective volunteers of Study GRC Inc. This document ensures the Organization collects necessary information to:
1.  Assess the applicant's skills and fit for remote roles.
2.  Conduct mandatory screening and due diligence for youth-serving positions in compliance with Texas Family Code and the Organization's *Child and Youth Safeguarding Policy*.
3.  Secure necessary legal acknowledgments regarding Intellectual Property (IP) assignment and liability waivers as required by the Bylaws.

### 2. Scope
*   **Applies to:** All individuals seeking to serve as volunteers, including committee members, mentors, content creators, and community moderators.
*   **Exclusions:** This form does not apply to candidates for the Board of Directors (who utilize the *Board Nomination Statement*) or paid employment applicants.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Applicant** | An individual submitting a request to perform unpaid services for the Organization. |
| **Sensitive Position** | A volunteer role involving direct interaction with minors (K-12), access to financial data, or administrative privileges on IT systems. |
| **Work Product** | As defined in Bylaws Article IX, Section 9.3, any content, code, or materials created by the volunteer during their service. |
| **Background Check** | A review of criminal and commercial records to determine eligibility for service. |

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Applicant** | Provide accurate, truthful, and complete information. Disclose any potential conflicts of interest or criminal history relevant to safety. |
| **Director of Kindness & Generosity (DKG)** | Review applications for cultural fit and skill alignment. Initiate the interview process. |
| **HR/Compliance Officer** | Review "Section III: Screening and Safety" responses. Initiate background checks for Sensitive Positions via the *Background Check Policy (HR-POL-024)*. |
| **Executive Director** | Final approval authority for Sensitive Position appointments. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
*   **Falsification:** Providing false or misleading information on this application is grounds for immediate disqualification or dismissal from volunteer service.
*   **Data Privacy:** All data collected via this form is processed in accordance with the *Master Data Privacy Policy (GOV-POL-070)*. Completed forms are confidential records restricted to HR and Legal personnel.
*   **Record Retention:** Applications of non-selected candidates are retained for one (1) year. Applications of active volunteers are retained for the duration of service plus seven (7) years, per the *Records Retention Schedule (GOV-STD-013)*.
