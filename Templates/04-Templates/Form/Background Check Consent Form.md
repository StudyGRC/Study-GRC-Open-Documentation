### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this document is to facilitate the legal collection of authorization from candidates (employees, volunteers, and independent contractors) to conduct background checks. This form ensures **Study GRC Inc.** complies with the **Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA) §604(b)** and relevant state laws regarding the procurement of consumer reports for employment and volunteer purposes. This process supports the Organization's commitment to youth protection, financial integrity, and the ethical standards mandated in **Bylaws Article III (Board Qualifications)** and **Article V (Operational Leadership)**.

### 2. Scope
This form applies to:
*   **Prospective and Current Employees:** Full-time, part-time, and temporary staff.
*   **Volunteers:** Specifically those with unsupervised access to minors, access to financial instruments, or administrative privileges on IT systems.
*   **Board of Directors:** As part of the vetting process for "Good Standing" (Bylaws §3.3).
*   **Independent Contractors:** Where stipulated by contract or the *Vendor Security Risk Assessment*.

**Exclusions:** This form is not required for general community participants or one-time event attendees who do not hold a position of trust or supervision.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Consumer Report** | Any written, oral, or other communication of information by a consumer reporting agency bearing on a consumer's credit worthiness, credit standing, credit capacity, character, general reputation, personal characteristics, or mode of living. |
| **Investigative Consumer Report** | A consumer report or portion thereof in which information on a consumer's character, general reputation, personal characteristics, or mode of living is obtained through personal interviews with neighbors, friends, or associates. |
| **CRA** | Consumer Reporting Agency. The third-party vendor authorized to conduct the background check. |
| **FCRA** | Fair Credit Reporting Act (15 U.S.C. § 1681 et seq.), federal law regulating the collection of consumers' credit information and access to their credit reports. |

### 4. Form Content and Requirements

*Note: To comply with FCRA §604(b)(2)(A), the Disclosure and Authorization language below must be presented to the candidate in a **stand-alone document**. It cannot be buried within an employment application or liability waiver.*

#### 4.1 Part I: Disclosure Regarding Background Investigation
**Study GRC Inc.** (the "Company") may obtain information about you from a third-party consumer reporting agency for employment or volunteer purposes. Thus, you may be the subject of a "consumer report" which may include information about your character, general reputation, personal characteristics, and/or mode of living. These reports may contain information regarding your criminal history, social security verification, motor vehicle records ("driving records"), verification of your education or employment history, or other background checks.

You have the right, upon written request made within a reasonable time, to request whether a consumer report has been run about you and to request a copy of your report. These searches will be conducted by:
*   **[Insert Name of CRA Vendor]**
*   **[Insert Vendor Address]**
*   **[Insert Vendor Phone/Website]**

#### 4.2 Part II: Authorization
I acknowledge that I have received and read the separate *Disclosure Regarding Background Investigation* and the *Summary of Your Rights Under the Fair Credit Reporting Act* (provided separately). I hereby authorize **Study GRC Inc.** to obtain "consumer reports" and/or "investigative consumer reports" about me at any time after receipt of this authorization and throughout my employment or service, if applicable.

I acknowledge that the information procured may be used to determine my eligibility for:
1.  Employment or continued employment;
2.  Volunteer service, specifically regarding eligibility to work with youth (K-12) populations;
3.  Access to financial systems or sensitive data.

To this end, I hereby authorize, without reservation, any law enforcement agency, administrator, state or federal agency, institution, school or university (public or private), information service bureau, employer, or insurance company to furnish any and all background information requested by the CRA acting on behalf of **Study GRC Inc.**

I agree that a facsimile ("fax"), electronic, or photographic copy of this Authorization shall be as valid as the original.

#### 4.3 Part III: State-Specific Provisions
*   **California, Minnesota, and Oklahoma Applicants Only:** Please check the box below if you would like to receive a copy of the consumer report if one is obtained by the Company.
    *   [ ] I request a copy of my consumer report.

*   **Washington State Applicants Only:** You also have the right to request from the consumer reporting agency a written summary of the rights and remedies available to you under the Washington Fair Credit Reporting Act.

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Candidate (Applicant/Volunteer)** | Provide truthful, accurate, and complete information. Sign the authorization form voluntarily. |
| **Hiring Manager / Volunteer Coordinator** | Ensure the candidate receives the *Summary of Rights* and that this form is signed *before* any background check is initiated. |
| **Human Resources (HR)** | Securely transmit data to the CRA. Review results against the *Background Check Adjudication Matrix*. Maintain confidentiality of results. |
| **Executive Director** | Final authority on adverse actions resulting from background checks for senior leadership or high-risk roles. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
*   **False Information:** Providing false, misleading, or incomplete information on this form is grounds for immediate disqualification from employment or volunteer service, or termination if discovered after onboarding.
*   **Data Privacy:** All data collected via this form is subject to **HR-POL-042 (Employee Personnel File Management Policy)** and **IS-POL-001 (Master Information Security Policy)**. Completed forms must be stored in a secure, access-controlled repository separate from the general personnel file.
*   **Adverse Action:** If Study GRC Inc. intends to deny employment/service based on the report, the Organization must follow the **Pre-Adverse** and **Final Adverse Action** notice procedures defined in **HR-POL-024**.
