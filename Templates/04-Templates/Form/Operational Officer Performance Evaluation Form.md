### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this document is to establish a standardized framework for evaluating the performance of Operational Officers appointed under **Article V** of the Bylaws of Study GRC Inc. This evaluation ensures:
1.  **Accountability:** Officers are held responsible for their fiduciary duties and operational objectives.
2.  **Alignment:** Leadership actions remain consistent with the Organization’s charitable purpose (Bylaws Art. I) and open-source commitment.
3.  **Development:** Officers receive constructive feedback to enhance their professional growth and effectiveness within the GRC community.

### 2. Scope
This evaluation applies exclusively to **Operational Officers** as defined in **Bylaws Article V, Section 5.2**, including but not limited to:
*   Executive Director (CEO)
*   Vice President of Education & Training
*   Director of Kindness and Generosity (DKG)
*   Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)
*   Chief Financial Officer (CFO)

**Exclusions:** This form is not for use with general volunteers, contractors, or non-officer staff.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Operational Officer** | An individual appointed by the Board to manage day-to-day execution of the mission (Bylaws Art. V). |
| **Review Period** | The specific timeframe under evaluation, typically the preceding fiscal year (Jan 1 – Dec 31). |
| **Fiduciary Duty** | The legal obligation to act in the best interest of the Organization, including duties of care, loyalty, and obedience. |
| **KPI** | Key Performance Indicator; a quantifiable measure used to evaluate success in meeting objectives. |

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Governance Committee** | Maintains this form and schedules annual reviews for all Operational Officers. |
| **Board of Directors** | Conducts the evaluation for the Executive Director (CEO). Reviews evaluations for other officers. |
| **Executive Director** | Conducts evaluations for direct reports (e.g., CISO, VP Education) and submits to the Board for ratification. |
| **Operational Officer** | Completes a self-evaluation using this form prior to the formal review meeting. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
Failure to participate in the performance evaluation process may result in the non-renewal of the Officer's term.

If an Officer receives an overall rating of **"Needs Improvement" (2)** or lower, a formal **Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)** must be established within 30 days. Failure to meet the terms of the PIP may result in removal from office via a majority vote of the Board (Bylaws Art. III, Sec 3.6) or by Member Petition (Bylaws Art. V, Sec 5.3).
