### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this document is to standardize the collection of information from individuals seeking election or appointment to the Board of Directors of Study GRC Inc. (the "Organization"). This form ensures compliance with **Bylaws Article II, Section 2.5**, which mandates that all nominees provide a "Nomination Statement" including their background, position statement, and a full disclosure of potential conflicts of interest. This document serves as the primary tool for Voting Members to evaluate candidates based on merit, transparency, and alignment with the Organization’s mission.

### 2. Scope
This form applies to:
*   All individuals nominated for election to the Board of Directors during the Annual Meeting.
*   All individuals appointed to fill a vacancy on the Board of Directors under **Bylaws Article III, Section 3.8**.

**Exclusions:** This form is not required for non-fiduciary roles such as Advisory Board members or Committee volunteers, unless they are seeking a voting Director seat.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Nominee** | An individual proposed for election to the Board of Directors. |
| **Conflict of Interest (COI)** | A situation where a person in a position of trust has a competing professional or personal interest that makes it difficult to act impartially. |
| **Fiduciary Duty** | The legal obligation to act in the best interest of the Organization, prioritizing its mission over personal gain. |
| **Work Product** | As defined in **Bylaws Article IX, Section 9.3**, content, code, and materials created during service to the Organization. |

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Nominee** | Complete the form truthfully and submit it to the Secretary by the nomination deadline (at least 14 days before the Annual Meeting). |
| **Secretary** | Review the form for completeness and eligibility. Distribute the form to Voting Members at least 10 days prior to the election. |
| **Governance Committee** | Review COI disclosures (Section 4) to determine if a conflict exists that would preclude service or require a management plan. |
| **Voting Members** | Review the Nomination Statement to make an informed vote. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement

**Disqualification:** Failure to submit this form by the deadline established in the Election Notice will result in the automatic disqualification of the nominee.

**False Statements:** If a Director is found to have knowingly made false statements on this form—specifically regarding Conflicts of Interest or Criminal History—it shall constitute "Cause" for removal. Removal proceedings will be initiated immediately pursuant to **Bylaws Article III, Section 3.6** (Removal by Board) or **Section 5.3** (Removal by Members).
