### 1. Purpose
The purpose of the **Personnel Activity Report (PAR)** is to provide a mandatory mechanism for the after-the-fact certification of effort expended by employees of Study GRC Inc. This document ensures compliance with **2 CFR § 200.430 (Compensation—personal services)**, which requires that charges to Federal awards for salaries and wages be based on records that accurately reflect the work performed. This form serves as the primary evidence to support salary distribution among specific activities or cost objectives.

### 2. Scope
This requirement applies to **all employees** of Study GRC Inc. who meet *any* of the following criteria:
1.  Are compensated, in whole or in part, by Federal grant funds.
2.  Work on more than one cost objective (e.g., split time between a Federal grant and general administration, or between two different Federal grants).
3.  Work on a Federal award and a non-Federal award.

**Exclusions:**
*   Independent Contractors (invoiced via Accounts Payable).
*   Volunteers (no compensation).
*   Employees funded 100% by non-federal, unrestricted general operating funds who do not touch federal projects.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Cost Objective** | A specific grant, contract, project, service, or other activity (such as "General Administration" or "Fundraising") to which costs are assigned. |
| **Effort** | The amount of time spent on any activity expressed as a percentage of the individual's **Total Professional Activity** for the organization. |
| **Total Professional Activity** | 100% of the time an employee is compensated for, regardless of the number of hours worked (e.g., 40 hours or 50 hours both equal 100% effort). |
| **After-the-Fact** | A determination made *after* the work period has ended, confirming what actually happened, rather than what was budgeted. |

### 4. Instructions and Form Requirements

#### 4.1 Frequency and Timing
*   **Frequency:** PARs must be completed **monthly**.
*   **Deadline:** Reports must be signed and submitted to the Finance Department no later than the **10th business day** following the close of the month.
*   **Budget vs. Actual:** Employees must report **actual** effort, not budgeted effort. If the actual effort deviates from the budgeted salary distribution by more than 5%, a budget adjustment or salary reallocation must be processed by the CFO.

#### 4.2 Form Completion Guidelines
1.  **Total Effort:** The sum of all activities must equal **100%**.
2.  **Granularity:** Effort must be broken down by specific Grant ID or Functional Area (e.g., Admin, Fundraising).
3.  **Signatures:** The form must be signed by the **Employee** AND a **Supervisory Official** having first-hand knowledge of the work performed.

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Employee** | Tracks time accurately; completes the PAR monthly; ensures total effort equals 100%; signs to certify accuracy. |
| **Supervisor** | Reviews the PAR against the employee's actual output; verifies accuracy; countersigns the form. |
| **Chief Financial Officer (CFO)** | Reviews PARs for mathematical accuracy; reconciles PAR percentages against payroll charges; processes journal entries to correct any variances between budgeted and actual effort. |
| **Treasurer** | Conducts spot-checks of PARs during quarterly internal audits to ensure compliance with Bylaws Article VIII (Records & Finance). |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
Failure to complete and submit this form accurately and on time places Study GRC Inc. at significant financial and legal risk.

*   **Financial Risk:** Salary costs unsupported by a PAR may be deemed "unallowable" by auditors, requiring the Organization to repay those funds to the Federal government.
*   **Legal Risk:** Intentionally falsifying this document constitutes a violation of the **False Claims Act (31 U.S.C. §§ 3729–3733)** and may result in criminal prosecution, fines, and debarment from federal funding.
*   **Disciplinary Action:** Employees or supervisors who fail to submit PARs or falsify data are subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.
