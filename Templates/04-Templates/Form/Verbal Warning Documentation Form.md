### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this form is to formally document a **Verbal Warning** issued to an employee or volunteer of Study GRC Inc. While the warning is delivered verbally, written documentation is required to ensure clarity, mutual understanding of expectations, and to create a record within the Organization’s **Progressive Discipline Framework**. This form serves as evidence that the individual was put on notice regarding specific performance deficiencies or behavioral issues.

### 2. Scope
This form applies to all **Employees** and **Volunteers** of Study GRC Inc. It is to be used by Managers, Directors, or Officers when initiating the first formal stage of disciplinary action.

**Exclusions:** This form is not used for immediate termination offenses (e.g., gross misconduct, youth protection violations, theft) which bypass the verbal warning stage pursuant to the *Progressive Discipline Policy*.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Verbal Warning** | A formal disciplinary conversation where a manager explicitly informs a subordinate that their conduct or performance is unacceptable. Unlike casual coaching, this is a recorded step in the disciplinary process. |
| **Progressive Discipline** | A process of increasingly severe consequences for repeated policy violations or failure to improve performance (e.g., Verbal Warning → Written Warning → Termination). |
| **At-Will Employment** | The legal doctrine in Texas stating that employment may be terminated by either the employer or employee at any time, for any reason (not prohibited by law), or for no reason at all. |

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Issuing Manager** | Conducts the private meeting, completes this form objectively, ensures the employee understands the expectations, and submits the form to HR. |
| **Employee/Volunteer** | Participates in the discussion, acknowledges receipt of the warning, and adheres to the corrective action plan. |
| **HR / Executive Director** | Reviews the form for consistency with organizational policy and retains the document in the personnel file in accordance with the *Records Retention Schedule*. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
This form is an internal record of Study GRC Inc.
*   **At-Will Disclaimer:** The issuance of this warning or the use of the progressive discipline system does not alter the "At-Will" employment relationship defined in the *Employee Handbook* and Texas Labor Code. Study GRC Inc. reserves the right to terminate employment at any time, with or without prior warnings, depending on the severity of the infraction.
*   **Confidentiality:** This document contains sensitive personnel information and must be handled in accordance with the *Employee Personnel File Management Policy*.
