### 1. Purpose
The purpose of the **Medical Treatment Authorization Form** is to grant Study GRC Inc. (the "Organization") and its designated agents the legal authority to seek, authorize, and consent to emergency medical treatment for a participant in the event of injury or illness during Organization-sponsored activities. This form ensures that critical medical care is not delayed due to a lack of consent, particularly when a parent or legal guardian is not immediately present or reachable.

### 2. Scope
*   **Mandatory:** This form must be completed and signed for all **Minors** (individuals under the age of 18) participating in any in-person event, workshop, or program hosted by the Organization.
*   **Recommended:** This form is highly recommended for **Adult** volunteers and participants to facilitate emergency response.
*   **Exclusions:** This form does not apply to purely virtual/remote participation where Organization staff have no physical proximity to the participant.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Participant** | The individual (adult or minor) attending the Study GRC Inc. event. |
| **Guardian** | A parent, managing conservator, or legal guardian with the authority to consent to medical treatment for a minor under Texas Family Code § 32.001. |
| **Emergency Medical Care** | Medical services required for the immediate diagnosis and treatment of medical conditions that, if not immediately diagnosed and treated, could lead to serious physical or mental disability or death. |
| **Designated Agent** | An operational officer, event lead, or safety officer of Study GRC Inc. authorized to act on behalf of the Organization. |

### 4. Instructions and Privacy

#### 4.1 Instructions for Completion
1.  **Minors:** A parent or legal guardian must complete all sections and sign.
2.  **Adults:** The participant may complete the form themselves.
3.  **Submission:** Forms must be submitted to the Event Safety Officer prior to the start of the activity.

#### 4.2 Data Privacy and Retention
*   **Confidentiality:** This form contains sensitive personal and medical information. It will be kept in a secure, physical or encrypted digital folder accessible **only** to the Event Safety Officer and the Executive Director.
*   **Usage:** Information will only be disclosed to emergency medical personnel (EMS, doctors, nurses) in the event of a medical emergency.
*   **Destruction:** Unless an incident occurs requiring the use of this form, this document will be securely destroyed (shredded or permanently deleted) 30 days after the conclusion of the event, in accordance with the *Data Retention and Destruction Policy*.

### 6. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Participant / Guardian** | Responsible for providing accurate, up-to-date medical information and emergency contact details. Responsible for all medical costs incurred. |
| **Event Safety Officer** | Responsible for collecting forms, verifying completion for all minors, maintaining physical security of the forms during the event, and presenting forms to EMS if required. |
| **Executive Director** | Responsible for ensuring this form is legally reviewed periodically and that data destruction protocols are followed post-event. |

### 7. Compliance and Enforcement
*   **Mandatory Requirement:** Failure to submit a completed Medical Treatment Authorization Form for a minor participant will result in the **denial of entry** to the event or program.
*   **Privacy Violation:** Unauthorized access, disclosure, or casual viewing of the medical information contained in this form by Study GRC staff or volunteers is a violation of the *Master Data Privacy Policy* and may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination.
