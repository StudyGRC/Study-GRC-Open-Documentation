### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this document is to initiate the **Interactive Process** required by the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) and the Texas Labor Code. It serves as the official mechanism for employees, volunteers, and applicants to request adjustments or modifications to their work environment or duties to enable them to perform **Essential Functions** of their role. This form ensures Study GRC Inc. meets its legal obligations and fulfills its mission of fostering an inclusive community.

### 2. Scope
This form applies to:
*   All current employees (full-time, part-time, and temporary).
*   Job applicants requiring accommodation during the interview/hiring process.
*   Volunteers and Board Members requiring accessibility support to perform their duties.

**Exclusions:** This form is not for general grievances or non-disability-related workplace preferences.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Disability** | A physical or mental impairment that substantially limits one or more major life activities, a record of such an impairment, or being regarded as having such an impairment. |
| **Reasonable Accommodation** | A modification or adjustment to a job, the work environment, or the way things are usually done that enables a qualified individual with a disability to enjoy an equal employment opportunity. |
| **Essential Functions** | The fundamental job duties that an individual must be able to perform, with or without reasonable accommodation. |
| **Interactive Process** | A collaborative dialogue between the employer and the individual to identify the precise limitations resulting from the disability and potential reasonable accommodations that could overcome those limitations. |
| **Undue Hardship** | An action requiring significant difficulty or expense when considered in light of factors such as an employer's size, financial resources, and the nature and structure of its operation. |

### 4. Procedures and Form Content

#### 4.1 Submission Instructions
1.  **Complete:** The requester must complete Sections A, B, and C of the form below.
2.  **Submit:** Submit this form directly to the **Director of Kindness and Generosity (DKG)** or the **Executive Director**. Do not submit this form to your immediate supervisor if you prefer to keep specific medical details confidential from them (supervisors will be informed of *limitations* and *accommodations*, but not necessarily *diagnoses*).
3.  **Next Steps:** Upon receipt, Leadership will contact you within five (5) business days to schedule a meeting to discuss your request (The Interactive Process).

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Requester** | Provide accurate information regarding limitations and participate in the interactive process in good faith. |
| **Director of Kindness & Generosity (DKG)** | Receive requests, maintain confidentiality, and facilitate the interactive process as defined in **Bylaws Article V**. |
| **Executive Director** | Final authority on determinations involving financial expenditure or "Undue Hardship" assessments. |
| **Supervisors** | Implement approved accommodations; strictly maintain confidentiality regarding the employee's medical information. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
Failure to engage in the interactive process in good faith may result in a delay or denial of the accommodation. Retaliation against an individual for requesting an accommodation is strictly prohibited by **Study GRC Inc. Anti-Harassment Policy** and federal law.

Information collected via this form is stored in a separate confidential medical file, distinct from the personnel file, in accordance with the **Record Retention Schedule (HR-STD-013)** and **GDPR/HIPAA** privacy standards.
