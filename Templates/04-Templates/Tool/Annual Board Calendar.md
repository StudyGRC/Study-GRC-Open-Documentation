### 1. Purpose
The purpose of the Annual Board Calendar is to ensure Study GRC Inc. (the "Organization") systematically fulfills its fiduciary, legal, and regulatory obligations. This tool operationalizes the requirements set forth in the Bylaws, Texas Business Organizations Code (TBOC), and Internal Revenue Service (IRS) regulations. It serves as the primary mechanism to prevent governance drift, ensure timely regulatory filings, and maintain the Organization’s 501(c)(3) tax-exempt status.

### 2. Scope
This calendar applies to the **Board of Directors**, **Board Officers**, **Committee Chairs**, and the **Executive Director**. It dictates the mandatory agenda items and filing deadlines for the Organization’s governance cycle.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Fiscal Year** | The financial operating period of the Organization, defined in Bylaws Article VIII, Section 8.3 as the calendar year (January 1 – December 31). |
| **Annual Meeting** | The mandatory meeting of Voting Members held in January, as required by Bylaws Article II, Section 2.5. |
| **Form 990** | The federal information return that tax-exempt organizations must file annually with the IRS. |
| **PMR** | Periodic Management Report (Texas), required by the Texas Secretary of State. |
| **Fundamental Action** | Significant corporate events (mergers, dissolution, asset sales) requiring supermajority approval per Bylaws Article X. |

### 4. Annual Governance Cycle

The Board shall adhere to the following schedule. Dates refer to the standard deadline; if a date falls on a weekend or holiday, the action is due the following business day.

#### 4.1 Quarter 1: Governance & Strategy (Jan – Mar)

| Month | Activity / Requirement | Bylaw/Legal Ref. | Responsible |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **January** | **Annual Meeting of Members**<br>• State of the Organization Address<br>• Presentation of Board Nominees<br>• Member Q&A<br>• **Election Window Opens** (7 days post-meeting) | Art. II, § 2.5 | President / Secretary |
| **January** | **Issue 1099s** to Contractors (Deadline: Jan 31) | IRS Regs | Treasurer / CFO |
| **February** | **Post-Election Board Meeting**<br>• Seating of New Directors<br>• **Election of Officers** (President, Secretary, Treasurer)<br>• Appointment of Committee Chairs | Art. IV, § 4.3 | Board of Directors |
| **February** | **Annual Conflict of Interest Disclosure**<br>• All Directors/Officers must sign. | Art. IX, § 9.1 | Secretary |
| **March** | **Annual Financial Report Approval**<br>• Review prior year financials.<br>• Approve report for transparency publication. | Art. VIII, § 8.2 | Treasurer |
| **March** | **Texas Franchise Tax Report Prep**<br>• Confirm "No Tax Due" status for non-profits. | TX Tax Code | CFO / Treasurer |

#### 4.2 Quarter 2: Compliance & Finance (Apr – Jun)

| Month | Activity / Requirement | Bylaw/Legal Ref. | Responsible |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **April** | **IRS Form 990 Review**<br>• Board review of draft Form 990.<br>• **Filing Deadline:** May 15. | IRS § 6033 | Treasurer / Board |
| **April** | **Quarterly Financial Review** (Q1 Actuals vs. Budget) | Fiduciary Duty | Finance Committee |
| **May** | **Texas Periodic Report** (If due - every 4 years)<br>• Verify Registered Agent/Office. | TX BOC § 22.357 | Secretary |
| **June** | **Insurance Portfolio Review**<br>• D&O, General Liability, Cyber Liability renewal assessment. | Risk Mgmt | Executive Director |
| **June** | **Mid-Year Strategic Review**<br>• Assess progress against Strategic Plan KPIs. | Governance | President |

#### 4.3 Quarter 3: Policy & Risk (Jul – Sep)

| Month | Activity / Requirement | Bylaw/Legal Ref. | Responsible |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **July** | **Quarterly Financial Review** (Q2 Actuals vs. Budget) | Fiduciary Duty | Finance Committee |
| **August** | **Policy Manual Review**<br>• Review/Update Governance Policies (Bi-annual cycle).<br>• Verify Document Retention compliance. | Art. XII | Governance Committee |
| **September** | **Executive Director Performance Evaluation**<br>• Initiate annual review process. | Art. V | Board (Executive Session) |
| **September** | **Single Audit Prep** (If Federal expenditures >$750k)<br>• Engage independent auditor if threshold met. | 2 CFR 200.501 | Audit Committee |

#### 4.4 Quarter 4: Planning & Succession (Oct – Dec)

| Month | Activity / Requirement | Bylaw/Legal Ref. | Responsible |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **October** | **Budget Planning**<br>• Draft budget for upcoming Fiscal Year presented. | Art. VIII | Treasurer / CFO |
| **October** | **Quarterly Financial Review** (Q3 Actuals vs. Budget) | Fiduciary Duty | Finance Committee |
| **November** | **Budget Approval**<br>• Final vote on upcoming FY Budget. | Fiduciary Duty | Board of Directors |
| **November** | **Board Self-Assessment**<br>• Evaluate Board effectiveness and skills gaps. | Best Practice | Governance Committee |
| **December** | **Nominations Committee**<br>• Call for Nominations (Open 14 days prior to Jan meeting).<br>• Vet candidates against "Good Standing" criteria. | Art. II, § 2.5 | Secretary |
| **December** | **Meeting Notice Distribution**<br>• Send notice for January Annual Meeting (10-60 days prior). | Art. II, § 2.5 | Secretary |

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **President** | Ensures agenda items for each month are included in the Board Packet; leads strategic reviews. |
| **Secretary** | Maintains the official calendar; issues meeting notices in compliance with Bylaws Article II; ensures minutes reflect the completion of calendar items. |
| **Treasurer** | Prepares financial reports and tax filings (Form 990) in alignment with the calendar deadlines; oversees the budgeting process. |
| **Governance Committee** | Reviews this calendar annually to ensure alignment with changing laws (e.g., changes to 2 CFR 200 or Texas BOC). |
| **Executive Director** | Provides operational data and reports required for Board review items (e.g., insurance, KPIs, budget drafts). |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement

**Failure to adhere to this calendar may result in:**
1.  **Legal Penalties:** Late filing fees from the IRS or Texas Secretary of State.
2.  **Loss of Status:** Revocation of 501(c)(3) tax-exempt status for failure to file Form 990 for three consecutive years.
3.  **Governance Failure:** Inability to seat a valid Board or elect Officers, rendering corporate acts potentially voidable.
4.  **Grant Ineligibility:** Failure to complete Single Audits (if applicable) results in suspension from Federal funding (2 CFR 200).

The Secretary shall report any deviations from this calendar to the Board immediately.
