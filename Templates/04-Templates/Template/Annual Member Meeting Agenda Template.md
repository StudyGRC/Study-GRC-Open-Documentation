### 1. Purpose
This document establishes the mandatory structure for the Annual Meeting of the Voting Members of Study GRC Inc. Its purpose is to ensure compliance with **Article II, Section 2.5** of the Organization's Bylaws and the **Texas Business Organizations Code (TBOC)**. It ensures that all statutory requirements—including quorum verification, financial reporting, and director candidate presentations—are executed in a consistent, transparent, and legally defensible manner.

### 2. Scope
This template applies to the **Annual Meeting** of the membership held in January of each year.
*   **Applies to:** Board of Directors, Officers, Meeting Organizers, and Voting Members.
*   **Exclusions:** This template is not intended for Regular Board Meetings or Special Emergency Meetings of the Board.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Quorum** | A majority (more than 50%) of the total number of Voting Members entitled to vote (Bylaws § 2.4). |
| **Voting Member** | A Supporting Member or Honorary Member in good standing as of the Record Date (Bylaws § 2.1). |
| **Record Date** | The specific date established by the Board to determine which members are eligible to receive notice and vote. |
| **Fundamental Action** | Significant events requiring a 2/3 member vote, such as mergers, dissolution, or amending the Certificate of Formation (Bylaws § 10.1). |

### 4. Template and Procedures

#### 4.1 Pre-Meeting Compliance Checklist
*   [ ] **Notice Sent:** Written notice delivered 10 to 60 days prior to the meeting date (Bylaws § 2.5).
*   [ ] **Voting List Prepared:** Alphabetical list of eligible voters prepared and available for inspection (Bylaws § 2.6).
*   [ ] **Candidate Statements:** Nomination statements and conflict disclosures collected for all Board nominees.
*   [ ] **Virtual Platform:** Secure video conferencing link tested; "Waiting Room" enabled for identity verification.

**I. CALL TO ORDER & PROCEDURAL MATTERS**
*   **1.1 Call to Order:** The President calls the meeting to order.
*   **1.2 Quorum Verification:** The Secretary certifies the number of Voting Members present (virtually).
    *   *Script:* "The total number of eligible Voting Members is [Number]. We have [Number] members present. A quorum [is/is not] established pursuant to Bylaws Section 2.4."
*   **1.3 Approval of Minutes:** Motion to approve the minutes from the previous Annual Meeting.
*   **1.4 Review of Agenda:** Overview of the meeting flow.

**II. REPORTS OF OFFICERS**
*   **2.1 President’s Report:**
    *   State of the Organization.
    *   Review of Strategic Plan progress.
    *   Highlights of community impact (Education, Open Source contributions).
*   **2.2 Treasurer’s Report (Annual Financial Report):**
    *   **Mandatory:** Presentation of the financial report for the preceding year as required by **TBOC § 22.352** and **Bylaws § 8.2**.
    *   Review of Statement of Support, Revenue, Expenses, and Changes in Fund Balances.
    *   *Note:* This report must be made available to members (e.g., via screen share or download link).

**III. ELECTION OF DIRECTORS**
*   **3.1 Explanation of Process:** The Secretary outlines the election rules:
    *   Number of open seats.
    *   Terms of office (2 years, staggered).
    *   Prohibition of proxy voting (Bylaws § 2.3).
*   **3.2 Candidate Presentations:**
    *   **Mandatory:** Pursuant to **Bylaws § 2.5**, each nominee is granted a strict **five (5) minute window** to present their qualifications and vision.
    *   *Order of Presentation:* [Alphabetical by Last Name].
    *   *Candidate 1:* [Name]
    *   *Candidate 2:* [Name]
    *   *Candidate 3:* [Name]
*   **3.3 Candidate Q&A:**
    *   A moderated Question and Answer period where members may query the candidates.
    *   *Time Limit:* [e.g., 15 minutes total].

**IV. NEW BUSINESS / FUNDAMENTAL ACTIONS**
*   *Note: Only items explicitly stated in the Meeting Notice may be voted upon if they are Special/Fundamental actions.*
*   **4.1 [Item Title]:** Presentation and discussion of any Fundamental Actions (e.g., Bylaw amendments requiring member approval).

**V. MEMBER OPEN FORUM**
*   **5.1 Community Feedback:** Open floor for members to ask questions or provide feedback to the Board.
    *   *Protocol:* Members must use the "Raise Hand" feature. Comments limited to [2] minutes.

**VI. VOTING INSTRUCTIONS & ADJOURNMENT**
*   **6.1 Voting Window Declaration:**
    *   The President announces the opening of the electronic voting window.
    *   *Script:* "In accordance with Bylaws Section 2.5, the formal election period begins immediately upon adjournment and will remain open for **seven (7) days** via [Name of Secure Voting System]."
*   **6.2 Adjournment:** Motion to adjourn the meeting.

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Presiding Officer (President)** | Chairs the meeting, maintains order, ensures the agenda is followed, and manages time limits for candidate presentations. |
| **Secretary** | Verifies member eligibility against the Record Date, establishes quorum, records minutes, and manages the electronic voting setup. |
| **Treasurer** | Presents the Annual Financial Report in compliance with TBOC § 22.352. |
| **Tech Host (IT/CISO)** | Manages the virtual platform, admits members from the waiting room after verification, and handles muting/unmuting during Q&A. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
*   **Statutory Requirement:** Failure to present the Annual Financial Report (Item 2.2) is a violation of Texas law and Organization Bylaws.
*   **Election Integrity:** Failure to provide candidates the mandated 5-minute presentation window or the subsequent Q&A period (Item 3.2/3.3) renders the election process non-compliant with Bylaws Section 2.5 and may be grounds for invalidating the election results.
*   **Quorum:** No business may be transacted (other than adjournment) if a quorum is not present.
