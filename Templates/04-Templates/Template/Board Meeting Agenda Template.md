### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this document is to establish a standardized framework for the Board of Directors meetings of Study GRC Inc. ("the Organization"). This template ensures that meetings are conducted efficiently, comply with the Organization's Bylaws, and adhere to *Robert's Rules of Order Newly Revised (RONR)*. A consistent agenda structure facilitates proper record-keeping, ensures all necessary business is transacted, and respects the time of volunteer Directors.

### 2. Scope
This standard applies to all **Regular Meetings** of the Board of Directors as defined in Bylaws Article III, Section 3.4.
*   **Inclusions:** Monthly Board meetings, quarterly strategy sessions.
*   **Exclusions:** Committee meetings (which may use a simplified format), Special Emergency Meetings (which are limited to the specific purpose stated in the notice), and the Annual Meeting of Members (governed by Bylaws Article II).

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Call to Order** | The official declaration by the Presiding Officer that the meeting has begun. |
| **Quorum** | The minimum number of Directors required to be present to transact business. Per Bylaws Section 3.5, this is a majority of Directors then in office. |
| **Consent Agenda** | A meeting practice which groups routine reports, minutes, and other non-controversial items into a single agenda item to be approved by one vote without discussion. |
| **Executive Session** | A portion of the meeting closed to non-Directors (and non-essential staff) to discuss confidential matters such as legal advice, personnel issues, or security audits. |
| **Unfinished Business** | Matters formally postponed from a previous meeting or items that were pending when the previous meeting adjourned. |

### 4. Agenda Construction Standard & Template

The Secretary, in coordination with the President, must prepare the agenda using the following structure. The agenda must be distributed to Directors at least **three (3) business days** prior to the meeting, accompanied by the Board Packet (minutes, financial reports, and proposals).

#### 4.1 Standard Order of Business
The agenda shall follow the standard order established by *Robert's Rules of Order*:
1.  Reading and Approval of Minutes
2.  Reports of Officers, Boards, and Standing Committees
3.  Reports of Special (Select) Committees
4.  Special Orders
5.  Unfinished Business and General Orders
6.  New Business

#### 4.2 The Agenda Template
The following template must be used for all Regular Board Meetings. Text in brackets `[...]` indicates fields to be populated prior to distribution.

**Study GRC Inc.**

**Date:** [YYYY-MM-DD]
**Time:** [Start Time] – [End Time] (Time Zone)

*   **1.3 Technical Check:** Verification that all remote participants have functioning audio/video.

*   **2.1 Conflict of Interest Declaration:** Directors must disclose any conflicts regarding items on today’s agenda pursuant to Bylaws Section 9.1.
*   **2.2 Approval of Agenda:** Motion to approve the agenda as presented or amended.

**III. CONSENT AGENDA**
*(Items in this section are approved by a single motion. Any Director may request an item be removed for separate discussion.)*
*   **3.1 Approval of Minutes:** Draft minutes from [Date of Last Meeting].
*   **3.2 Routine Committee Reports:** [List specific reports, e.g., Governance Committee Report].
*   **3.3 Operational Updates:** [List specific reports, e.g., Monthly Membership Statistics].

**IV. FINANCIAL OVERSIGHT**
*   **4.1 Treasurer’s Report:** Presentation of the Statement of Financial Position and Statement of Activity.
*   **4.2 Budget Variance Analysis:** Review of actuals vs. budget.

**V. STRATEGIC BUSINESS (REPORTS & PRESENTATIONS)**
*   **5.1 Executive Director Report:** High-level overview of operations, major wins, and blockers.
*   **5.2 [Specific Strategic Topic]:** Presentation by [Name/Role] regarding [Topic].

**VI. UNFINISHED BUSINESS**
*(Items postponed from previous meetings)*
*   **6.1 [Item Name]:** [Brief description of the motion or discussion point].

**VII. NEW BUSINESS**
*(Action items requiring a vote)*
*   **7.1 Motion to [Action]:** Sponsored by [Director Name/Committee].
    *   *Context:* [Brief summary or reference to Board Packet page].
    *   *Proposed Action:* Vote to approve/deny.
*   **7.2 Motion to [Action]:** Sponsored by [Director Name/Committee].

**VIII. EXECUTIVE SESSION (Closed Door)**

*   **8.2 [Confidential Topic]**

**IX. ANNOUNCEMENTS & ROUNDTABLE**
*   **9.1 Upcoming Dates:** Next Board Meeting scheduled for [Date].
*   **9.2 Director Comments:** Open floor for brief comments (no formal business).

**X. ADJOURNMENT**
*   **10.1 Motion to Adjourn.**

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Secretary** | Responsible for drafting the agenda, collecting reports for the Board Packet, and distributing materials to Directors within the required timeframe. |
| **President** | responsible for reviewing and approving the final agenda prior to distribution and facilitating the meeting according to the agenda. |
| **Directors** | Responsible for reviewing the agenda and Board Packet prior to the meeting to ensure informed decision-making. Directors wishing to add items to "New Business" must notify the Secretary 48 hours in advance. |
| **Executive Director** | Responsible for providing operational reports and supporting data to the Secretary for inclusion in the agenda. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
Failure to adhere to this standard may result in inefficient meetings, failure to record necessary corporate actions, or challenges to the validity of votes taken on items not properly noticed.
*   **Bylaws Violation:** Failure to establish a Quorum (Item 1.2) renders any business transacted null and void.
*   **Transparency:** Failure to approve minutes (Item 3.1) violates Bylaws Article VIII, Section 8.1 regarding maintenance of records.
