### 1. Purpose
This document provides standardized response templates for handling Data Subject Access Requests (DSARs) submitted to Study GRC Inc. Its purpose is to ensure consistency, professionalism, and strict adherence to statutory timelines defined by the **Texas Data Privacy and Security Act (TDPSA)** and the **California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)**.

These templates are designed to facilitate compliance with **Bylaws Article IX (Policies & Data Protection)**, specifically Section 9.1 regarding transparency and data protection, ensuring that all Voting Members and Community Participants exercise their rights within the mandated 45-day window.

### 2. Scope
**Applies to:** All personnel (employees, volunteers, and contractors) responsible for monitoring the privacy inbox (`privacy@studygrc.org`) or processing consumer data requests.

**Exclusions:** These templates do not apply to law enforcement data requests (refer to *IS-PRC-045 Law Enforcement Data Request Procedure*).

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **DSAR** | Data Subject Access Request. A formal inquiry by an individual to exercise rights regarding their personal data (access, deletion, correction, or portability). |
| **Verified Request** | A request where the identity of the requester has been successfully matched to existing records (e.g., via email verification or member portal login). |
| **Statutory Window** | The legal timeframe to respond. Under TDPSA and CCPA, this is **45 calendar days** from receipt, extendable by an additional 45 days with notice. |
| **Cure Period** | The timeframe allowed to fix a violation before enforcement action is taken (30 days under CCPA; 30 days under TDPSA). |

### 4. Response Templates

The following templates must be customized based on the specific nature of the request. Text in `[brackets]` indicates variable data that must be filled in by the Privacy Team.

#### 4.1 Template A: Initial Acknowledgment (Send within 10 Days)
*Use this template immediately upon receiving a DSAR to confirm receipt and initiate the verification process.*

**Subject:** Acknowledgment of Your Data Privacy Request - Reference #[Ticket ID]

Dear [Consumer Name],

Thank you for contacting Study GRC Inc. regarding your personal data. We have received your request dated [Date of Request] regarding your [Right to Know / Right to Delete / Right to Correct].

**Next Steps:**
To protect your privacy and the security of our community data, we must verify your identity before processing this request.
1.  **If you are a Member:** Please reply to this email from the email address associated with your Study GRC membership account.
2.  **If you are a Guest/Visitor:** Please provide [Insert specific verification requirement, e.g., "confirmation of the last event attended" or "date of newsletter subscription"].

**Timeline:**
Under the Texas Data Privacy and Security Act (TDPSA) and/or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), we will respond to your verified request within **45 days**. If we require more time (up to an additional 45 days), we will inform you in writing with the reason for the extension.

If you have any questions, please reply to this email quoting Reference #[Ticket ID].

Sincerely,

The Privacy Team
Study GRC Inc.

#### 4.3 Template C: Request Fulfillment - Right to Delete
*Use this template after successfully deleting the user's data.*

**Subject:** Confirmation of Data Deletion - Reference #[Ticket ID]

Dear [Consumer Name],

Per your request dated [Date], Study GRC Inc. has successfully deleted your personal information from our active systems.

**Please Note:**
*   **Retained Data:** Certain information may be retained where required by law (e.g., financial transaction records for IRS compliance) or for security purposes (e.g., keeping your email on a suppression list to ensure we do not contact you again).
*   **Backups:** Residual copies of your data may exist in our backup systems. These are securely isolated and will be overwritten in due course according to our *Data Retention and Destruction Policy*.

If you have a Study GRC membership, this action has terminated your account and access to the community platform.

Sincerely,

The Privacy Team
Study GRC Inc.

#### 4.4 Template D: Notice of Extension (45-Day Delay)
*Use this template if the request is complex and cannot be fulfilled within the initial 45 days.*

We will provide our final response no later than [Date = Initial Date + 90 Days].

The Privacy Team

#### 4.5 Template E: Denial of Request
*Use this template if identity cannot be verified or if an exemption applies.*

**Subject:** Decision Regarding Your Data Request - Reference #[Ticket ID]

Dear [Consumer Name],

We are writing regarding your request dated [Date]. After a thorough review, we are unable to fulfill your request at this time for the following reason(s):

*   [ ] **Inability to Verify Identity:** We were unable to verify your identity to a reasonable degree of certainty based on the information provided. To protect the security of our community members, we cannot release or delete data without verified authorization.
*   [ ] **Exemption:** The request pertains to data that is exempt under the law (e.g., data necessary for legal defense or compliance with federal record-keeping laws).
*   [ ] **Excessive/Repetitive:** This request is manifestly unfounded or excessive in nature.

**Right to Appeal (TDPSA):**
If you are a resident of Texas, you have the right to appeal this decision. To submit an appeal, please reply to this email with the subject line "Appeal of Privacy Decision" and explain the basis for your appeal. We will respond to appeals within 60 days.

Sincerely,

Chief Information Security Officer
Study GRC Inc.

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Requester (Data Subject)** | Submits the initial request and provides necessary information to verify identity. |
| **Privacy Team / DPO** | Monitors the privacy inbox, customizes these templates, ensures identity verification is complete, and tracks the 45-day timeline. |
| **IT / Systems Admin** | Executes the technical data extraction or deletion upon instruction from the Privacy Team. |
| **CISO** | Approves any "Denial of Request" or "Notice of Extension" prior to sending. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
Failure to utilize these templates or adhere to the 45-day statutory timeline may result in:
1.  **Regulatory Penalties:** Fines from the Texas Attorney General (up to $7,500 per violation) or California Privacy Protection Agency.
2.  **Internal Discipline:** Disciplinary action for staff/volunteers up to and including termination, as per the *Employee Handbook*.
