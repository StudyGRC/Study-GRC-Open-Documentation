### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this document is to establish the standardized format and legal requirements for extending offers of employment at Study GRC Inc. This template ensures compliance with **Texas Labor Code**, federal **Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA)** regulations, and the Organization’s **Bylaws**. It is designed to clearly articulate the terms of employment, reinforce the "At-Will" nature of the relationship, and protect the Organization's Intellectual Property rights.

### 2. Scope
*   **Applies to:** All offers of employment for W-2 positions (Full-Time, Part-Time, Temporary) extended by Study GRC Inc.
*   **Exclusions:** This template must **not** be used for Independent Contractors (1099), Volunteers, or unpaid Interns. Refer to *HR-CON-001 Independent Contractor Agreement* or *HR-VOL-002 Volunteer Service Agreement* for those classifications.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **At-Will Employment** | A legal doctrine in Texas stating that either the employer or the employee may terminate the employment relationship at any time, for any reason (or no reason), with or without notice, provided the reason is not illegal. |
| **Conditional Offer** | An offer of employment that is contingent upon the successful completion of specific requirements, such as a criminal background check or reference verification. |
| **Exempt vs. Non-Exempt** | Classifications under the FLSA determining eligibility for overtime pay. Non-Exempt employees are entitled to overtime; Exempt employees are not. |
| **Remote-First** | The operational model of Study GRC Inc. where the primary worksite is the employee's approved remote location. |

### 4. Template Standards and Content

All employment offer letters issued by Study GRC Inc. must adhere to the following template structure. Text in brackets `[ ]` indicates variable data that must be customized by the Hiring Manager prior to issuance.

#### 4.1 Mandatory Template Text

***

**[Date]**

**[Candidate Name]**
**[Candidate Address]**
**[Candidate Email]**

**Re: Offer of Employment**

Dear **[Candidate Name]**,

Study GRC Inc. (the "Organization") is pleased to offer you the position of **[Job Title]**. We are impressed with your qualifications and believe your skills will be a valuable asset to our mission of fostering a community-driven GRC ecosystem.

This letter outlines the terms and conditions of your employment.

**1. Position and Reporting**
*   **Title:** [Job Title]
*   **Department:** [Department Name]
*   **Reporting To:** [Manager Name and Title] (Note: Per Bylaws Article V, operational roles ultimately report up to the Executive Director).
*   **Start Date:** [Start Date]
*   **Status:** [Full-Time / Part-Time]
*   **FLSA Classification:** [Exempt (Salaried) / Non-Exempt (Hourly)]

**2. Compensation**
*   **Salary/Rate:** You will be paid a [Salary/Hourly Rate] of **$[Amount]** per [Year/Hour].
*   **Pay Schedule:** Wages will be paid [Bi-Weekly / Monthly] in accordance with the Organization’s standard payroll schedule and Texas Payday Law.
*   **Payment Method:** All payments are made via Direct Deposit.

**3. Remote Work and Location**
As a Remote-First organization, your primary worksite will be your home office located at **[City, State]**. You are expected to maintain a workspace that meets the safety and security standards outlined in the *HR-POL-005 Remote Work Policy* and *IS-POL-001 Information Security Policy*.

**4. Benefits**
You will be eligible to participate in the Organization’s benefit plans generally available to employees of your status, subject to the terms and conditions of those plans. Details regarding these benefits are provided in the *Employee Handbook*. The Organization reserves the right to modify or terminate benefit plans at any time.

**5. At-Will Employment Relationship**
Your employment with Study GRC Inc. is **"At-Will."** This means that either you or the Organization may terminate the employment relationship at any time, for any reason, or no reason at all, with or without cause or advance notice. This "At-Will" status cannot be modified by any oral or written statements, policies, or practices, except by a written agreement signed by the Executive Director.

**6. Contingencies**
This offer is conditional upon:
*   **Background Check:** Successful completion of a criminal background check, specifically required for our youth-serving programs (K-12 focus).
*   **Work Authorization:** Proof of your identity and authorization to work in the United States as required by the Immigration Reform and Control Act (Form I-9).
*   **Reference Checks:** Satisfactory verification of professional references.

**7. Intellectual Property and Confidentiality**
In accordance with **Article IX, Section 9.3 of the Bylaws of Study GRC Inc.**, all work product created by you during your employment is considered a "work made for hire." You agree to assign all rights, title, and interest in such work to the Organization. Additionally, you will be required to sign a *Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)* and an *Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)* prior to your start date to protect member data and organizational assets.

**8. Policies**
By accepting this offer, you agree to adhere to all Organization policies, including but not limited to the *Code of Ethics*, *Conflict of Interest Policy*, and *Child Safeguarding Policy*.

**9. Acceptance**
To accept this offer, please sign and date this letter and return it to **[HR Contact Name]** by **[Expiration Date]**.

We are excited to welcome you to the team and look forward to your contributions to Study GRC Inc.

Sincerely,

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Hiring Manager** | Populates the template with specific role details (Title, Salary, Reporting Line) and submits to HR for review. |
| **Human Resources** | Reviews the drafted offer for compliance with the approved budget and FLSA classification. Issues the final letter to the candidate. |
| **Executive Director** | Holds final signature authority for all employment offers. |
| **Candidate** | Reviews, signs, and returns the offer letter to initiate the onboarding process. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
*   **Modification:** Any deviation from the "At-Will" language or the "Intellectual Property" clauses in this template requires written approval from Legal Counsel and the Board of Directors.
*   **Record Retention:** Signed offer letters must be retained in the employee’s Personnel File for the duration of employment plus 4 years, in accordance with *HR-STD-013 Records Retention Schedule*.
*   **Failure to Comply:** Issuing an offer letter outside of this approved template may result in disciplinary action against the issuer and may render the offer voidable by the Organization.
