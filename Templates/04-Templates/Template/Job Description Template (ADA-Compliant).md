### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this document is to establish a standardized, legally compliant format for creating job descriptions within Study GRC Inc. This template ensures compliance with the **Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA)** by clearly distinguishing between "Essential Functions" and "Marginal Functions." It also supports compliance with the **Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA)** regarding overtime exemption status and aligns with the Organization's **Bylaws Article V** regarding Operational Leadership roles.

### 2. Scope
This template must be used for all employment positions, independent contractor roles, and long-term volunteer appointments within the Organization.
*   **Applies to:** All Departments (Education, Operations, IT/Security, Finance).
*   **Exclusions:** Short-term, task-based micro-volunteering (e.g., one-time event setup).

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Essential Functions** | The fundamental job duties that an individual must be able to perform, with or without reasonable accommodation. A function is essential if the position exists to perform that function. |
| **Marginal Functions** | Duties that are incidental to the main purpose of the job. These can be reassigned or redistributed if necessary to accommodate a disability. |
| **Reasonable Accommodation** | A modification or adjustment to a job, the work environment, or the way things are usually done that enables a qualified individual with a disability to enjoy an equal employment opportunity. |
| **FLSA Exempt** | Employees paid on a salary basis who meet specific duty tests and are not eligible for overtime pay. |
| **Youth Protection Level** | A classification (Level 1-3) determining the depth of background checks and supervision required based on interaction with minors. |

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Hiring Manager** | Drafts the job description using this template, ensuring "Essential Functions" are accurate and non-discriminatory. |
| **Executive Director** | Reviews and approves the final job description to ensure budget alignment and organizational need. |
| **HR / Operations** | Verifies FLSA status and ADA compliance; archives the signed description in the Personnel File. |
| **Employee/Candidate** | Reviews and signs the description, acknowledging ability to perform essential functions with or without accommodation. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
Failure to utilize this template for new hires may result in inconsistent employment practices and increased legal liability. Job descriptions that do not accurately reflect "Essential Functions" may complicate the Organization's ability to defend against ADA discrimination claims or deny unemployment claims for cause.
