### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this Standard is to objectively define the criteria for "Good Standing" within Study GRC Inc. As stipulated in **Article II, Section 2.2** of the Bylaws, voting rights and eligibility for Board service are strictly contingent upon a member maintaining Good Standing. This document eliminates ambiguity regarding voter eligibility, quorum calculation, and access to corporate records.

### 2. Scope
This Standard applies to all classes of membership defined in **Article II, Section 2.1** of the Bylaws, with specific emphasis on:
*   **Supporting Members (Voting)**
*   **Honorary Members (Voting)**
*   **Directors and Officers** (who must maintain membership Good Standing to serve per **Article III, Section 3.3**).

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Good Standing** | The status assigned to a member who has met all financial obligations, administrative requirements, and behavioral standards of the Organization. Only members in Good Standing may vote. |
| **Arrears** | The state of having failed to pay required membership dues by the specified due date. |
| **Grace Period** | A defined timeframe (30 days) following a due date during which a member retains Good Standing despite non-payment. |
| **Suspension** | A temporary revocation of Good Standing resulting from disciplinary action or prolonged financial delinquency. |
| **Record Date** | The specific date established by the Board for determining which members are eligible to receive notice of a meeting and to vote. |

### 4. Standards for Good Standing

To be considered in "Good Standing," a member must satisfy **all** of the following criteria simultaneously. Failure to meet any single criterion results in the immediate loss of Good Standing and associated rights.

#### 4.1 Financial Standing
*   **4.1.1 Dues Payment:** Supporting Members must have paid all applicable membership dues in full.
*   **4.1.2 Grace Period:** A member remains in Good Standing for a Grace Period of **thirty (30) calendar days** following their membership renewal date.
*   **4.1.3 Delinquency:** On the 31st day following a missed payment, the member is automatically deemed "Not in Good Standing" without further action by the Board.
*   **4.1.4 Honorary Exemption:** Honorary Members are exempt from financial dues requirements but must adhere to all other standards defined herein.

#### 4.2 Ethical and Behavioral Standing
*   **4.2.1 Code of Conduct:** The member must not be currently subject to any disciplinary sanctions, suspension, or expulsion proceedings regarding violations of the *Code of Ethics and Conduct* or the *Anti-Harassment Policy*.
*   **4.2.2 Investigation Status:** A member under active investigation for a "Major Violation" (as defined in the *Member Expulsion Due Process Policy*) may have their Good Standing provisionally suspended by a majority vote of the Board pending the investigation outcome.

#### 4.3 Administrative Standing
*   **4.3.1 Contact Information:** The member must maintain a valid, functioning email address on file with the Organization.
*   **4.3.2 Bounce Rule:** If official Organization notices (e.g., Meeting Notices, Ballots) are returned as "Undeliverable" or "Hard Bounce" from the member's primary email address, the member is deemed unreachable and is temporarily removed from Good Standing until updated contact information is provided. This ensures quorum calculations are not skewed by "ghost" accounts.

#### 4.4 Impact on Rights (Rights Contingency)
A member designated as "Not in Good Standing" immediately loses the following rights until status is restored:
*   **Voting Rights:** Ineligibility to cast votes in annual elections, special meetings, or fundamental actions (**Bylaws Art. II, §2.2**).
*   **Nomination Rights:** Ineligibility to nominate candidates or run for a seat on the Board of Directors (**Bylaws Art. III, §3.3**).
*   **Inspection Rights:** Ineligibility to inspect the books and records of the Organization (**Bylaws Art. II, §2.6**; **TBOC § 22.351**).
*   **Quorum Count:** The member shall not be counted toward the total number of voting members for the purpose of establishing a quorum (**Bylaws Art. II, §2.4**).

#### 4.5 Restoration of Good Standing (Cure)
*   **Financial Cure:** A member may restore Good Standing immediately upon payment of all dues in arrears.
*   **Administrative Cure:** A member may restore Good Standing immediately upon verifying a functional email address with the Secretary.
*   **Disciplinary Cure:** Good Standing is restored only upon the expiration of a suspension term or by specific resolution of the Board following a disciplinary review.

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Secretary** | Maintains the official Membership Roll; certifies the list of members in Good Standing as of the Record Date for all votes. |
| **Treasurer** | Monitors dues payments; notifies the Secretary of members in arrears beyond the Grace Period. |
| **Board of Directors** | Sets the Record Date for elections; adjudicates disciplinary suspensions affecting Good Standing. |
| **Member** | Responsible for ensuring dues are paid and contact information is current. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
The Secretary shall conduct a "Good Standing Audit" no less than **ten (10) days** prior to any Annual Meeting or Special Meeting to generate the certified voter list.

Any member attempting to vote while not in Good Standing will have their ballot disqualified. Falsely asserting Good Standing to gain access to confidential records or voting systems constitutes a violation of the *Code of Ethics* and may result in termination of membership.
