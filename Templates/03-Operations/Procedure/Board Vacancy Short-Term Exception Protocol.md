### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this procedure is to operationalize **Article III, Section 3.8** of the Bylaws of Study GRC Inc. regarding the filling of Board vacancies. Specifically, this document defines the criteria and steps for invoking the "Short Term Exception," allowing a Board seat to remain vacant until the next scheduled Annual Meeting. This protocol ensures that the Organization maintains statutory compliance regarding minimum Board composition while avoiding the administrative burden of conducting special elections for terms with limited remaining duration.

### 2. Scope
This procedure applies to the Board of Directors and is triggered immediately upon the resignation, removal, death, or incapacitation of a serving Director.

**Exclusions:**
*   This procedure does not apply if the vacancy reduces the total number of Directors to fewer than three (3). In such cases, the **Board Vacancy Minimum Requirement Override (GOV-PRC-042)** applies immediately.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Unexpired Term** | The period of time between the effective date of the vacancy and the date of the next scheduled Annual Meeting of the Membership. |
| **Short-Term Threshold** | A period of less than three (3) calendar months (approx. 90 days). |
| **Minimum Composition** | The statutory and Bylaw requirement (Article III, Section 3.2) that the Board must consist of no fewer than three (3) Directors. |
| **Special Election** | An ad-hoc election process initiated outside of the standard Annual Meeting cycle to fill a vacancy. |

### 4. Procedures

#### 4.1 Vacancy Assessment
Upon receipt of a resignation notice or confirmation of a vacancy, the Secretary must perform an immediate assessment:

1.  **Determine Effective Date:** Confirm the official date the seat became vacant.
2.  **Calculate Unexpired Term:** Calculate the number of days between the Effective Date and the next scheduled Annual Meeting.
3.  **Verify Board Count:** Count the number of remaining active Directors.

#### 4.2 Minimum Composition Check (Mandatory)
Before considering the Short-Term Exception, the Board must verify compliance with **Article III, Section 3.8 (Minimum Requirement Override)**.

1.  If the remaining Director count is **fewer than three (3)**:
    *   The Short-Term Exception is **void**.
    *   The Board **must** immediately initiate the **Board Vacancy Filling Procedure (GOV-PRC-040)** or the **Emergency Succession Plan**, regardless of how little time remains in the term.
2.  If the remaining Director count is **three (3) or more**:
    *   Proceed to Section 4.3.

#### 4.3 Applying the Short-Term Exception
If the Minimum Composition Check (4.2) is passed, the Board shall apply the time-based criteria:

1.  **Scenario A: Unexpired Term is Three (3) Months or More**
    *   The Short-Term Exception **does not apply**.
    *   The Board must initiate a Special Election to fill the vacancy per **GOV-PRC-040**.
2.  **Scenario B: Unexpired Term is Less Than Three (3) Months**
    *   The Board possesses the **discretion** to leave the seat vacant.
    *   The President shall call for a motion to invoke the Short-Term Exception.
    *   **Vote:** A majority vote of the remaining Directors is required to formally leave the seat vacant.
    *   **Rationale:** If the Board determines that a Special Election is necessary for operational continuity despite the short timeframe, they may choose to hold an election. However, the default preference is to invoke the exception to preserve resources.

#### 4.4 Documentation and Notification
Once the decision is made:

1.  **Minutes:** The Secretary must record the vacancy, the calculation of the unexpired term, the verification of the minimum director count, and the Board's vote to invoke the exception in the meeting minutes.
2.  **Membership Notice:** The Organization is not required to hold a vote, but must notify the Voting Members that the seat will remain vacant until the upcoming Annual Meeting. This notice may be combined with the standard Notice of Annual Meeting.

#### 4.5 Consolidation with Annual Election
1.  The vacant position shall be added to the slate of open positions for the upcoming Annual Election.
2.  The term for the newly elected Director filling this seat will align with the standard staggering schedule defined in the Bylaws.

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Secretary** | Calculates the unexpired term; verifies minimum board count; records the decision in the official minutes. |
| **President** | Calls the meeting to address the vacancy; facilitates the motion to invoke the exception. |
| **Board of Directors** | Votes to formally invoke the Short-Term Exception or to proceed with a Special Election. |
| **Governance Committee** | Reviews the vacancy timeline to ensure the "Short Term" definition is applied consistently. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
Failure to strictly adhere to the Minimum Composition Check (Section 4.2) may result in the Organization falling out of good standing with the Texas Secretary of State and violating IRS 501(c)(3) governance requirements.

Any decision to leave a seat vacant for a period longer than three (3) months without an election is a violation of **Bylaws Article III, Section 3.8** and may be subject to challenge by the Voting Membership.
