### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this checklist is to establish a mandatory protocol for verifying the identity of participants in official governance meetings of Study GRC Inc. held via electronic means. This document ensures compliance with **Texas Business Organizations Code (TBOC) § 6.002** and **§ 22.002**, which require the Organization to implement reasonable measures to verify that every person voting or participating is, in fact, the member or director they purport to be. This protocol mitigates the risks of unauthorized access, fraudulent voting, and "Zoom-bombing."

### 2. Scope
This checklist applies to all **Governance Meetings** conducted remotely or via hybrid methods, including:
*   Annual Meetings of the Membership.
*   Special Meetings of the Membership.
*   Regular and Special Meetings of the Board of Directors.

**Exclusions:** This checklist does not apply to general public webinars, open community training sessions, or informal committee working sessions where binding votes are not taken.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Roster** | The official list of Voting Members or Directors entitled to notice and vote, prepared in accordance with Bylaws Section 2.6. |
| **Gatekeeper** | The designated individual (usually the Secretary or a technical moderator) responsible for managing the virtual waiting room and admitting participants. |
| **Unverified Participant** | A user attempting to join the meeting with an unrecognizable display name (e.g., "iPhone", "User123") or without prior registration. |
| **Visual Verification** | Confirmation of identity via live video feed compared against known likeness or identification. |

### 4. Verification Procedures & Checklist

The Gatekeeper and Secretary must utilize the following checklist for every Governance Meeting.

#### 4.1 Phase I: Pre-Meeting Preparation
| Status | Action Item | Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| &#9744; | **Generate Official Roster** | Prepare the alphabetical list of entitled voters (Bylaws § 2.6) including email addresses and voting status (Supporting/Honorary). |
| &#9744; | **Configure Meeting Security** | Enable "Waiting Room" feature. Disable "Join Before Host." Configure meeting to mute participants upon entry. |
| &#9744; | **Registration Requirement** | Ensure meeting links are unique to registered users (if platform allows) or require registration matching the email on file. |

#### 4.2 Phase II: Admission (The Waiting Room)
*To be performed by the Gatekeeper before admitting users from the Waiting Room.*

| Status | Action Item | Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| &#9744; | **Display Name Audit** | Verify that the participant's display name matches a name on the Official Roster. |
| &#9744; | **Rename Protocol** | If a display name is generic (e.g., "Samsung Galaxy"), message the user in the Waiting Room (if supported) or admit individually to immediate "Breakout Room" for triage. |
| &#9744; | **Cross-Reference Email** | If the platform displays the authenticated email, match it against the Member Record. |
| &#9744; | **Proxy Check** | **STRICT ENFORCEMENT:** Ensure no single individual is attempting to log in multiple times or on behalf of another. **Proxy voting is prohibited** (Bylaws § 2.3). |

#### 4.3 Phase III: Active Verification (Quorum Call)
*To be performed immediately upon calling the meeting to order.*

| Status | Action Item | Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| &#9744; | **Video Confirmation** | Request all Directors/Members to briefly enable cameras for visual verification by the Secretary. |
| &#9744; | **Audio Challenge** | If a participant cannot enable video, the Chair must perform a verbal challenge (e.g., "Please state your full name and the date of your membership approval for the record"). |
| &#9744; | **Quorum Calculation** | Count only **Verified Participants**. Update the Quorum count in the minutes. |
| &#9744; | **Lock Meeting** | Once Quorum is established and the grace period (e.g., 15 minutes) has passed, the Host may lock the meeting to prevent unverified late entry, subject to Board discretion. |

#### 4.4 Phase IV: Voting Integrity
*To be performed during any motion requiring a vote.*

| Status | Action Item | Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| &#9744; | **Voter Eligibility Check** | Ensure only those with "Voting" status (Supporting/Honorary) have access to the ballot. "Community Participants" (Non-Voting) must be segregated or restricted from the polling tool. |
| &#9744; | **Ballot Reconciliation** | Ensure the number of cast votes does not exceed the number of Verified Participants present. |

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Secretary** | Ultimate authority on Member eligibility. Maintains the Official Roster. Certifies the Quorum. |
| **Gatekeeper / Host** | Technical execution of the Waiting Room. Admits only verified users. Removes disruptive or unverified users. |
| **Chair (President)** | Calls for the identity verification (Roll Call). Directs the removal of unverified participants if they fail to identify themselves. |
| **Participant** | Responsible for using their real legal name as their display name and enabling audio/video when requested for verification. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
Failure to adhere to this checklist may result in:
1.  **Invalidation of Votes:** Any vote cast by an unverified entity may be challenged and voided.
2.  **Quorum Challenge:** Failure to verify identities may render the meeting's Quorum invalid under TBOC § 22.002.
3.  **Removal:** Participants who refuse to identify themselves or who falsify their identity will be immediately removed from the digital meeting space and may face disciplinary action under the *Member Expulsion Due Process Policy*.
