### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this checklist is to ensure that all involuntary employment terminations at Study GRC Inc. are conducted lawfully, consistently, and fairly. This document serves as a mandatory due diligence tool to mitigate legal risks associated with wrongful termination, discrimination, retaliation, and defamation claims. It validates that the decision to terminate is supported by adequate documentation and aligns with the Organization’s *At-Will* employment status under Texas law.

### 2. Scope
This checklist applies to the involuntary termination of all **paid employees** of Study GRC Inc.

**Exclusions:**
*   **Volunteer Dismissals:** Refer to *Volunteer Termination/Dismissal Procedure (HR-PRC-022)*.
*   **Board Directors:** Removal is governed strictly by **Bylaws Article III, Section 3.6**.
*   **Operational Officers:** While this checklist applies to the employment aspect, removal of an Officer appointed by the Board may require specific Board or Member action pursuant to **Bylaws Article V, Section 5.3**.
*   **Independent Contractors:** Governed by the termination clauses within their specific Service Agreements.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **At-Will Employment** | The legal doctrine in Texas allowing either the employer or employee to terminate the employment relationship at any time, for any reason (or no reason), provided the reason is not illegal (e.g., discrimination). |
| **Protected Class** | Groups protected from employment discrimination by law (e.g., race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, genetic information). |
| **Protected Activity** | Actions taken by an employee that are legally protected from retaliation, such as filing a harassment complaint, requesting FMLA leave, or whistleblowing. |
| **PIP** | Performance Improvement Plan; a formal document outlining performance deficiencies and required corrective actions. |
| **Operational Officer** | Senior leadership roles defined in **Bylaws Article V** (e.g., VP of Education, CISO, CFO). |

### 4. Termination Review Checklist

**Instructions:** This checklist must be completed by the Direct Manager and reviewed by HR/Legal Counsel **BEFORE** the termination meeting occurs.

| [ ] | **Officer Status Check:** | Is the employee an Operational Officer per **Bylaws Art. V**? If YES, has the Board been notified/approved? |

#### 4.2 Section B: Documentation & Performance History
| Check | Item | Notes/Verification |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| [ ] | **Offer Letter/Contract Review:** | Does the employee have a written contract overriding "At-Will" status? (Check specifically for "For Cause" requirements). |
| [ ] | **Job Description Alignment:** | Was the employee clearly informed of the expectations they failed to meet? |
| [ ] | **Prior Disciplinary Actions:** | Are there signed warnings (Verbal, Written, Final) in the personnel file? |
| [ ] | **Performance Improvement Plan (PIP):** | Was a PIP issued? Did the employee fail to meet the specific objectives within the timeline? |
| [ ] | **Evidence of Misconduct:** | If for misconduct, is there concrete evidence (logs, emails, witness statements, investigation report)? |
| [ ] | **Employee Explanations:** | Has the employee been given a chance to explain their side (if applicable)? |

#### 4.3 Section C: Legal Risk Assessment (The "Red Flags")
*If any item in this section is checked "YES", Legal Counsel consultation is **MANDATORY** before proceeding.*

| Yes | No | Risk Factor |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| [ ] | [ ] | **Protected Class:** Is the employee a member of a protected class where an inference of discrimination could be drawn? |
| [ ] | [ ] | **Recent Complaints:** Has the employee filed a formal or informal complaint (harassment, safety, ethics) in the last 6 months? |
| [ ] | [ ] | **Whistleblower Status:** Has the employee reported a violation of law or policy under the *Whistleblower Protection Policy (GOV-POL-009)*? |
| [ ] | [ ] | **Leave Status:** Is the employee currently on, or recently returned from, FMLA, ADA, or Military leave? |
| [ ] | [ ] | **Accommodation Request:** Has the employee recently requested a reasonable accommodation for a disability or religious practice? |
| [ ] | [ ] | **Workers' Compensation:** Has the employee recently filed a Workers' Comp claim? |
| [ ] | [ ] | **Timing:** Is the termination occurring suspiciously close in time to any of the above events? |

#### 4.4 Section D: Consistency & Fairness (Disparate Impact)
| Check | Item | Notes/Verification |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| [ ] | **Comparative Treatment:** | Have other employees committed similar infractions? Were they also terminated? (Avoid "selective enforcement"). |
| [ ] | **Policy Adherence:** | Are we following our own *Progressive Discipline Policy (HR-POL-048)*? If skipping steps, is the "Gross Misconduct" justification solid? |
| [ ] | **Code of Ethics:** | Does the termination align with the *Code of Ethics and Conduct*? |

#### 4.5 Section E: Logistics & Security (Remote-First Context)
| Check | Item | Notes/Verification |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| [ ] | **IT Access Revocation Plan:** | Has the CISO been notified to revoke access to Slack, Google Workspace, GitHub, and AWS at the exact time of termination? |
| [ ] | **Asset Retrieval:** | List of hardware (Laptops, YubiKeys) at the employee's home. Are prepaid shipping labels generated? |
| [ ] | **Data Security:** | Has a review been conducted to ensure no sensitive IP or member data was recently downloaded to personal devices? |
| [ ] | **Final Pay Calculation:** | Has Payroll prepared the final check, including accrued PTO (if required by policy/state law) and expense reimbursements? |
| [ ] | **Severance Agreement:** | Is a separation agreement with a release of claims being offered? (Drafted by Legal). |

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Direct Manager** | Compiles all documentation, performance logs, and evidence; initiates the checklist. |
| **HR Director** | Reviews the checklist for completeness, consistency, and policy adherence. Acts as the primary gatekeeper. |
| **Legal Counsel** | Reviews "Section C" risks; drafts severance agreements; advises on high-risk terminations. |
| **Executive Director** | Provides final authorization for the termination. |
| **CISO** | Executes the technical offboarding and access revocation to protect organizational data. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
This checklist is a mandatory internal control. Terminating an employee without completing and signing this review may result in disciplinary action against the manager responsible, up to and including termination.
