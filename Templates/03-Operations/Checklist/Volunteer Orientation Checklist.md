### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this checklist is to standardize the onboarding process for all volunteers at Study GRC Inc. This document ensures that every volunteer is legally vetted, operationally prepared, and fully trained on compliance requirements—specifically regarding Youth Protection and Data Privacy—before commencing service. This checklist serves as the primary record of "Preparedness" to mitigate organizational liability and ensure alignment with the nonprofit purposes defined in **Article I, Section 1.3** of the Bylaws.

### 2. Scope
This checklist applies to **all** prospective volunteers, including Board Directors, Advisory Board members, and operational volunteers.
*   **Tier 1 (General):** Administrative, content creation, and technical roles with no direct access to minors.
*   **Tier 2 (Youth-Facing):** Roles involving direct interaction (virtual or physical) with K-12 beneficiaries.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Volunteer** | An individual performing services for Study GRC Inc. without expectation of compensation, other than reimbursement for approved expenses. |
| **Youth-Facing** | Any role that involves direct communication, instruction, or supervision of minors (individuals under 18), including moderation of student Discord channels. |
| **Work Product** | As defined in **Bylaws Article IX, Section 9.3**, content, code, and materials created by volunteers, which are assigned to the Organization. |
| **Two-Deep Leadership** | A safety policy requiring a minimum of two authorized adults to be present during any interaction with a minor. |

### 4. Orientation Checklist Procedures

The Volunteer Coordinator or Supervising Manager must verify the completion of each item below.

#### 4.1 Phase I: Pre-Service Vetting & Administration
*Must be completed **before** access to systems is granted.*

*   [ ] **Application Review:** Confirm submission of the Volunteer Application Form (HR-FRM-021).
*   [ ] **Identity Verification:** Verify government-issued ID via video call (due to remote-first nature) to prevent identity fraud.
*   [ ] **Background Check Initiation:**
    *   [ ] Obtain signed FCRA Consent Form.
    *   [ ] Submit for criminal history and sex offender registry check (Required for **all** roles per Risk Management Policy).
    *   [ ] **Youth-Facing Only:** Verify clearance level meets "Enhanced" criteria for working with minors.
*   [ ] **Service Agreement Execution:**
    *   [ ] Signed **Volunteer Service Agreement & Liability Waiver**.
    *   [ ] Signed **Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)** per **Bylaws Article IX, Section 9.2**.
    *   [ ] Signed **Intellectual Property Assignment** acknowledging "Work Made for Hire" status per **Bylaws Article IX, Section 9.3**.

#### 4.2 Phase II: Governance & Mission Alignment
*   [ ] **Mission Briefing:** Review **Bylaws Article I**, emphasizing the commitment to "zero-cost, open-source resources."
*   [ ] **Conflict of Interest Disclosure:**
    *   [ ] Review **Conflict of Interest Policy (GOV-POL-005)**.
    *   [ ] Sign and file the **Annual Disclosure Statement** per **Bylaws Article IX, Section 9.1**.
*   [ ] **Whistleblower Policy:** Review reporting mechanisms for ethical violations or financial impropriety.
*   [ ] **Code of Conduct:** Review and sign the Professional Ethics and Community Guidelines.

#### 4.3 Phase III: Safety, Compliance & Security (Mandatory)
*   [ ] **Youth Protection Training (HR-TRN-005):**
    *   [ ] Review **Child and Youth Safeguarding Policy**.
    *   [ ] Complete "Mandated Reporter" training (Texas Family Code requirements).
    *   [ ] Acknowledge **Two-Deep Leadership** and "No 1-on-1 Private Messaging" rules.
*   [ ] **Data Privacy & GDPR/TDPSA Training:**
    *   [ ] Review **Master Data Privacy Policy**.
    *   [ ] Training on handling PII (Personally Identifiable Information) and Member data.
*   [ ] **Cybersecurity Awareness:**
    *   [ ] Review **Acceptable Use Policy (IS-POL-007)**.
    *   [ ] Configure **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)** on all assigned accounts (Google Workspace, Slack, Discord).
    *   [ ] Review Phishing and Social Engineering indicators.

#### 4.4 Phase IV: Operational Setup (Remote-First)
*   [ ] **Communication Tools:**
    *   [ ] Provision accounts (Email, Slack, Jira/Trello).
    *   [ ] Setup profile with professional photo and role title.
    *   [ ] Review "Async Communication" guidelines.
*   [ ] **Timekeeping:**
    *   [ ] Create account in the Volunteer Hours Tracking System.
    *   [ ] Explain the importance of logging hours for **Federal Grant Match** requirements (2 CFR 200.306).
*   [ ] **Home Office Safety:**
    *   [ ] Complete **Remote Worksite Safety Self-Assessment** (Ergonomics and physical safety).

#### 4.5 Phase V: Role-Specific Onboarding
*   [ ] **Job Description Review:** Clarify specific duties, deliverables, and reporting lines.
*   [ ] **Team Introduction:** Virtual introduction to the functional team (e.g., Education, IT, Marketing).
*   [ ] **Access Rights:** Grant "Least Privilege" access to specific folders or repositories necessary for the role.

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Director of Kindness & Generosity (DKG)** | Overall owner of the volunteer experience; ensures the checklist is updated and utilized. |
| **Volunteer Coordinator** | Executes the checklist; verifies background checks; maintains personnel files. |
| **Volunteer** | Completes all required forms, training modules, and disclosures in a timely manner. |
| **CISO / IT Dept** | Provisions access only upon receipt of confirmation that Phase I and Phase III are complete. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
Completion of this checklist is mandatory for all volunteers.
*   **No Access:** Volunteers may not be granted access to Study GRC Inc. systems, data, or community channels until **Phase I** and **Phase III** are verified complete.
*   **Termination:** Failure to complete the orientation within 14 days of the start date may result in the revocation of the volunteer offer.
*   **Audit Trail:** This completed checklist must be retained in the volunteer's personnel file for a minimum of 3 years post-service for liability and grant compliance auditing.
