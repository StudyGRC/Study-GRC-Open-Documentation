### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this checklist is to standardize the onboarding process for all new employees of Study GRC Inc. This document ensures compliance with Texas labor laws, federal grant requirements (2 CFR 200), and youth protection standards. It is designed to mitigate legal risks associated with employment eligibility, data security, and organizational culture integration while facilitating a seamless entry into the Organization’s remote-first environment.

### 2. Scope
*   **Applies to:** All newly hired W-2 employees (full-time and part-time) of Study GRC Inc.
*   **Exclusions:** Independent Contractors (1099), Volunteers, and Advisory Board members (refer to *Volunteer Onboarding Checklist* or *Contractor Engagement Protocol*).

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Pre-Boarding** | The period between the acceptance of the offer letter and the employee's first official day of work. |
| **I-9 Verification** | The mandatory process to verify the identity and employment authorization of individuals hired for employment in the United States. |
| **2 CFR 200** | Uniform Administrative Requirements, Cost Principles, and Audit Requirements for Federal Awards; governs timekeeping and compensation for grant-funded roles. |
| **Provisioning** | The IT process of setting up user accounts, access rights, and shipping hardware to the employee. |

### 4. Procedures (Checklist)

The Hiring Manager or HR Designee must ensure all steps are completed and documented in the employee’s personnel file.

#### 4.1 Phase I: Pre-Boarding (14 Days to 1 Day Before Start)
*   **4.1.1 Background Check Clearance**
    *   [ ] Initiate background check via designated vendor (Criminal, Sex Offender Registry).
    *   [ ] **Critical:** Verify clearance is received *before* granting access to any youth-related data or systems.
*   **4.1.2 IT Provisioning & Hardware**
    *   [ ] Order and ship laptop/peripherals to employee's remote address.
    *   [ ] Create Google Workspace/Email Account (`firstname.lastname@studygrc.org`).
    *   [ ] Configure Identity Management (SSO) and enforce Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA).
    *   [ ] Pre-load required security software (EDR, MDM profile).
*   **4.1.3 Documentation Prep**
    *   [ ] Send "Welcome Packet" email with start date, time, and Zoom link for Day 1 orientation.
    *   [ ] Distribute *Remote Work Agreement* for review.

#### 4.2 Phase II: Day 1 (Compliance & Legal)
*   **4.2.1 Employment Eligibility (I-9)**
    *   [ ] Complete USCIS Form I-9 Section 1 (Employee).
    *   [ ] Complete USCIS Form I-9 Section 2 (Employer/Authorized Rep) within 3 business days. *Note: Follow Remote Verification Procedure if applicable.*
*   **4.2.2 Payroll & Tax Forms**
    *   [ ] Complete IRS Form W-4.
    *   [ ] Complete Direct Deposit Authorization Form.
*   **4.2.3 Policy Acknowledgments (Digital Signature Required)**
    *   [ ] **Employee Handbook** (At-Will Employment Acknowledgment).
    *   [ ] **Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)** (Cybersecurity).
    *   [ ] **Conflict of Interest Policy** (Pursuant to Bylaws Article IX).
    *   [ ] **Child and Youth Safeguarding Policy** (Mandatory for all staff).
    *   [ ] **Remote Work Agreement & Home Office Safety Self-Assessment**.
    *   [ ] **Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)** (Pursuant to Bylaws Article IX, Section 9.2).
    *   [ ] **Intellectual Property Assignment** (Work Made for Hire).

#### 4.3 Phase III: Week 1 (Training & Access)
*   **4.3.1 Mandatory Compliance Training**
    *   [ ] Sexual Harassment Prevention Training.
    *   [ ] Cybersecurity Awareness & Phishing Simulation.
    *   [ ] **Mandated Reporter Training** (Texas Family Code Chapter 261).
    *   [ ] Data Privacy Training (GDPR/TDPSA basics).
*   **4.3.2 Federal Grant Compliance (If Applicable)**
    *   [ ] Training on *Time and Effort Reporting* (2 CFR 200.430).
    *   [ ] Review of *Allowable Costs Policy*.
*   **4.3.3 Operational Access**
    *   [ ] Grant access to specific Slack channels, Jira/Confluence, and shared drives based on "Least Privilege."
    *   [ ] Introduction to the *Director of Kindness and Generosity* for culture orientation.

#### 4.4 Phase IV: Month 1 (Integration)
*   **4.4.1 Performance Management**
    *   [ ] Review Job Description and set initial 30/60/90 day goals.
    *   [ ] Schedule recurring 1:1 meetings with Hiring Manager.
*   **4.4.2 Benefits Enrollment**
    *   [ ] Complete enrollment in health/dental/vision plans (if eligible).
    *   [ ] Review 401(k) or retirement options.

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Hiring Manager** | Initiates the request for hire; defines role-specific access needs; conducts culture orientation and goal setting. |
| **HR Manager** | Administers background checks; verifies I-9 compliance; maintains the personnel file; ensures all policy acknowledgments are signed. |
| **CISO / IT Dept** | Provisions hardware; creates accounts; enforces MFA; manages access control and security training assignment. |
| **New Employee** | Submits required documents timely; completes mandatory training; acknowledges all governance policies. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
Failure to complete the critical compliance steps of this checklist (specifically I-9 verification, Background Checks for youth-facing roles, and Policy Acknowledgments) may result in the revocation of the employment offer, immediate termination of employment, or delay in payroll processing.

**At-Will Employment Statement:** Completion of this checklist does not constitute an employment contract for a specified term. Employment with Study GRC Inc. remains at-will.
