### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this checklist is to ensure that all General and Special Meetings of the Voting Members of Study GRC Inc. are convened in strict accordance with **Article II, Section 2.5** of the Bylaws and the Texas Business Organizations Code (TBOC). Proper notice is a jurisdictional prerequisite; failure to provide compliant notice may render any actions taken, elections held, or policies adopted at the meeting null and void.

### 2. Scope
This checklist applies to the Board Secretary and Operational Leadership responsible for scheduling and announcing meetings of the Membership.
*   **Applies to:** Annual Meetings of Members, Special Meetings of Members.
*   **Exclusions:** Regular Board of Directors meetings, Committee meetings, and informal community gatherings.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Voting Member** | Supporting Members and Honorary Members in good standing as defined in Bylaws Article II, Section 2.1. |
| **Record Date** | The specific date established by the Board to determine which members are eligible to receive notice and vote. |
| **Fundamental Action** | A significant event (merger, dissolution, sale of assets) requiring a two-thirds vote and specific notice language per Bylaws Article X. |
| **Special Meeting** | Any meeting of the membership other than the designated Annual Meeting held in January. |

### 4. Compliance Checklist

The Secretary or designated officer must verify completion of the following steps.

#### 4.1 Phase I: Timeline Verification (Bylaws § 2.5)
*   **[ ] Establish Record Date:** Confirm the Board has set a "Record Date" to determine the list of eligible voters.
*   **[ ] Calculate Notice Window:**
    *   **Minimum:** Notice must be sent **at least 10 days** prior to the meeting date.
    *   **Maximum:** Notice must be sent **no more than 60 days** prior to the meeting date.
*   **[ ] Nomination Window (Annual Meeting Only):** Verify that the period for nominating Board candidates opened at least **14 days** before the Annual Meeting.

#### 4.2 Phase II: Content Requirements (Bylaws § 2.5)
The written notice (email) **MUST** contain the following elements:
*   **[ ] Date and Time:** Clearly state the date and start time (including Time Zone).
*   **[ ] Place / Virtual Access:**
    *   Since Study GRC Inc. is remote-first, provide the secure link (e.g., Zoom, Teams) and access credentials (password/token).
*   **[ ] Agenda / Purpose:**
    *   **Annual Meeting:** General agenda (e.g., "Election of Directors," "Annual Financial Report").
    *   **Special Meeting:** **MANDATORY:** State the *specific purpose* for which the meeting is called. No other business may be transacted.
*   **[ ] Candidate Information (If Election is held):**
    *   Include or link to the "Nomination Statement" for each candidate.
    *   Include or link to the Conflict of Interest disclosures for each candidate.
*   **[ ] Fundamental Action Disclosure (If applicable):**
    *   If the meeting involves a merger, dissolution, or amendment to the Certificate of Formation, the notice must explicitly state this and include a copy or summary of the proposed plan/amendment.

#### 4.3 Phase III: Delivery and Transparency (Bylaws § 2.6)
*   **[ ] Recipient Verification:** Notice sent to the email address on record for every Voting Member in good standing as of the Record Date.
*   **[ ] Voting List Preparation:**
    *   Prepare an alphabetical list of voting members entitled to notice (Name, Email, Vote Count).
    *   Ensure this list is available for electronic inspection by members starting **two (2) business days** after notice is sent and continuing through the meeting.
*   **[ ] Electronic Voting Instructions:**
    *   Include instructions on how the electronic voting window will operate (opening immediately after the meeting for 7 days).
    *   Explicitly state that **Proxy Voting is Prohibited** (Bylaws § 2.3).

#### 4.4 Phase IV: Documentation
*   **[ ] Proof of Notice:** Save a copy of the sent email (including timestamp and recipient list/count) to the corporate records.
*   **[ ] Affidavit of Mailing:** (Optional but recommended) The Secretary signs a statement certifying that notice was given in accordance with Bylaws.

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Secretary** | Primary owner of this checklist. Prepares the voting list, drafts the notice, and certifies delivery. |
| **President / Chair** | Approves the final agenda and ensures the "Purpose" for Special Meetings is accurately defined. |
| **IT / Operations** | Generates the Voting Member email list from the CRM/Database and ensures the virtual meeting platform is secure. |
| **Governance Committee** | Reviews candidate Nomination Statements for completeness prior to inclusion in the notice. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
Failure to strictly adhere to the 10-to-60-day notice window or failure to state the specific purpose of a Special Meeting constitutes a **governance failure**.
*   **Consequence:** Any vote taken at a meeting with defective notice is voidable under Texas law.
*   **Remedy:** The meeting must be rescheduled with new, compliant notice, resulting in operational delay and reputational damage.
