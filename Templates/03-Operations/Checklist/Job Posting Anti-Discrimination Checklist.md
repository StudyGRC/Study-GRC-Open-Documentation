### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this checklist is to ensure that all job advertisements, position descriptions, and volunteer vacancy announcements issued by Study GRC Inc. comply with federal and state equal employment opportunity laws, including Title VII of the Civil Rights Act of 1964, the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA), and the Age Discrimination in Employment Act (ADEA). This tool mitigates the risk of discriminatory hiring practices and ensures alignment with the Organization’s mission to foster an inclusive, community-driven ecosystem.

### 2. Scope
This checklist applies to **all** personnel responsible for drafting, reviewing, or publishing job postings for Study GRC Inc.
*   **In Scope:** All paid employment positions (Operational Officers, Staff), independent contractor roles, and volunteer appointments (including Advisory Board and Committee members).
*   **Exclusions:** This checklist does not apply to the election of Board Directors, which is governed strictly by Article II and Article III of the Bylaws.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Protected Class** | Groups of people protected from discrimination and harassment by law. Includes race, color, religion, sex (including pregnancy, sexual orientation, or gender identity), national origin, age (40 or older), disability, and genetic information. |
| **Essential Functions** | The fundamental job duties that an individual must be able to perform, with or without reasonable accommodation. |
| **Disparate Impact** | Employment practices that are facially neutral in their treatment of different groups but that in fact fall more harshly on one group than another and cannot be justified by business necessity. |
| **BFOQ** | Bona Fide Occupational Qualification. A very rare legal exception where a specific characteristic is necessary for the normal operation of the business (e.g., a female actor for a female role). |

### 4. Compliance Checklist

All job postings must be reviewed against the following criteria prior to publication. Any item marked "No" requires immediate revision.

#### 4.1 Language and Tone (Title VII & ADEA)
| Criteria | Yes | No | N/A |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Gender-Neutral Titles:** Job titles are gender-neutral (e.g., "Chair" instead of "Chairman," "Server" instead of "Waitress," "Salesperson" instead of "Salesman"). | [ ] | [ ] | [ ] |
| **Gender-Neutral Pronouns:** The description uses "they/them," "the candidate," or "you" rather than "he/she." | [ ] | [ ] | [ ] |
| **Avoidance of Gender-Coded Words:** The text avoids words that subtly discourage specific genders (e.g., avoid "ninja," "rockstar," "dominate" which may skew male; avoid "nurturing," "supportive" if used to stereotype female roles). | [ ] | [ ] | [ ] |
| **Age Neutrality:** The posting avoids terms that imply a preference for younger workers, such as "digital native," "recent graduate" (unless a specific fellowship), "energetic," or "young team." | [ ] | [ ] | [ ] |
| **Immigration Status:** The posting does not state "U.S. Citizens Only" unless required by a specific federal contract or law (per 8 U.S.C. § 1324b). Instead, use: "Must be authorized to work in the U.S." | [ ] | [ ] | [ ] |

#### 4.2 Accessibility and Physical Requirements (ADA)
| Criteria | Yes | No | N/A |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Focus on Outcome, Not Method:** Physical requirements describe the *outcome* rather than the specific physical action (e.g., "Ability to move 25 lbs" instead of "Ability to lift/carry 25 lbs"). | [ ] | [ ] | [ ] |
| **Essential vs. Marginal:** Physical requirements listed are strictly **Essential Functions**. Marginal (non-critical) physical tasks are excluded or labeled as such. | [ ] | [ ] | [ ] |
| **Sensory Neutrality:** Requirements avoid assuming specific sensory abilities unless strictly necessary (e.g., "Communicate effectively" instead of "Hear and speak"). | [ ] | [ ] | [ ] |
| **Remote Work Accessibility:** As a remote-first organization, the posting clarifies that the work is performed virtually, reducing physical barriers. | [ ] | [ ] | [ ] |

#### 4.3 Education and Experience (Disparate Impact)
| Criteria | Yes | No | N/A |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Degree Requirements:** Educational requirements (e.g., "Master's Degree") are strictly necessary for the role and not used as a general filter, which may cause disparate impact. "Or equivalent experience" is included where possible. | [ ] | [ ] | [ ] |
| **Experience Caps:** The posting does not set a maximum limit on experience (e.g., "No more than 10 years experience"), which discriminates against older workers. | [ ] | [ ] | [ ] |
| **Language Fluency:** "Native English Speaker" is avoided. Use "Fluency in English" or "Professional proficiency" instead. | [ ] | [ ] | [ ] |

#### 4.4 Organizational Mandates & Youth Protection
| Criteria | Yes | No | N/A |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Background Check Notice:** The posting explicitly states that an offer of employment/service is contingent upon the successful completion of a background check, in compliance with the *Background Check Policy* and *Child and Youth Safeguarding Policy*. | [ ] | [ ] | [ ] |
| **EEO Statement:** The official Equal Employment Opportunity statement is included at the bottom of the posting (see Section 4.5). | [ ] | [ ] | [ ] |
| **Remote Work Eligibility:** The posting specifies eligible geographic locations (e.g., "Remote - Texas Residents Only" or "Remote - US Based") to ensure compliance with tax nexus and labor laws. | [ ] | [ ] | [ ] |

#### 4.5 Mandatory EEO Statement
*Ensure the following text (or the current Board-approved version) is included verbatim:*

> "Study GRC Inc. is an Equal Opportunity Employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees and volunteers. We do not discriminate based upon race, religion, color, national origin, gender (including pregnancy, childbirth, or related medical conditions), sexual orientation, gender identity, gender expression, age, status as a protected veteran, status as an individual with a disability, or other applicable legally protected characteristics."

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Hiring Manager** | Drafts the job description and completes this checklist prior to submission for approval. |
| **HR / Compliance Officer** | Reviews the completed checklist and draft text; holds authority to reject postings that violate EEOC guidelines. |
| **Executive Director** | Provides final approval for all Operational Officer postings (per Bylaws Article V). |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
Failure to comply with this standard may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or volunteer assignment. Posting discriminatory job advertisements puts Study GRC Inc. at significant legal risk of EEOC investigation, fines, and reputational damage.
