### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this Remote Work Agreement is to define the terms and conditions under which employees and volunteers of Study GRC Inc. (the "Organization") perform their duties from a location other than a centralized office. This document establishes the legal framework to ensure compliance with the Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA), state Workers' Compensation laws, and the Organization’s Information Security standards. It clarifies the distinction between "work time" and "personal time" to mitigate liability and ensure operational efficiency.

### 2. Scope
This agreement applies to:
*   All employees (Exempt and Non-Exempt) authorized to work remotely.
*   Designated volunteers with access to Organization systems or data.
*   Contractors, where specified in their Master Service Agreement (MSA).

**Exclusions:** This agreement does not apply to ad-hoc, one-off instances of working from home due to inclement weather or temporary illness, which are governed by the *Employee Handbook*.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Remote Work Site** | The specific, designated area within the employee’s home or other approved location where work duties are performed. |
| **Non-Exempt Employee** | An employee subject to the minimum wage and overtime provisions of the FLSA (typically hourly). |
| **Exempt Employee** | An employee not subject to overtime requirements under the FLSA (typically salaried executive, administrative, or professional roles). |
| **Work Hours** | The specific timeframe during which the employee is expected to be reachable, productive, and performing job duties. |

### 4. Agreement Terms and Conditions

#### 4.1. Conditions of Employment
Remote work is a condition of employment for this "Remote-First" organization. However, the Organization retains the right to require attendance at in-person retreats, meetings, or events as defined by the Board or Executive Director.
*   **At-Will Employment:** This agreement does not alter the "at-will" nature of employment under Texas law. Either party may terminate the employment relationship at any time, with or without cause.
*   **Performance Standards:** The employee agrees to maintain the same level of productivity, availability, and quality of work as though they were in a physical office.

#### 4.2. FLSA Compliance and Work Schedules
To comply with the Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA):
*   **Fixed Schedule:** Employees must adhere to the work schedule agreed upon with their supervisor.
*   **Timekeeping (Non-Exempt):** Non-exempt employees are required to accurately record all hours worked using the Organization’s designated time-tracking system.
    *   **Off-the-Clock Work:** Performing work without recording time is strictly prohibited.
    *   **Overtime:** Non-exempt employees must obtain **written prior approval** from their supervisor before working more than 40 hours in a workweek. Working unauthorized overtime may result in disciplinary action, though the time will be compensated as required by law.
*   **Meal and Rest Breaks:** Employees must adhere to the *Meal and Rest Break Policy*, taking a bona fide meal period (typically 30-60 minutes) during which they are completely relieved of duties.

#### 4.3. Workers' Compensation and Safety
The Organization’s Workers' Compensation coverage applies to job-related injuries that occur in the **Remote Work Site** during defined **Work Hours**.
*   **Designated Workspace:** The employee agrees to designate a specific workspace (e.g., a home office desk) that is clean, safe, and free of obstructions.
*   **Liability Limitation:** The Organization is not liable for injuries to third parties (e.g., family members, visitors) at the Remote Work Site, nor for injuries to the employee that occur outside the designated workspace or outside of work hours.
*   **Incident Reporting:** In the event of a job-related injury, the employee must report the incident to their supervisor and HR immediately (within 24 hours) to facilitate claims processing.
*   **Safety Inspection:** The Organization reserves the right to inspect the Remote Work Site (virtually via video call or, with notice, in person) to ensure it meets safety and ergonomic standards.

#### 4.4. Information Security and Confidentiality
Pursuant to **Bylaws Article IX (Policies & Data Protection)** and the direction of the Chief Information Security Officer (CISO):
*   **Data Privacy:** The employee must ensure that the Remote Work Site allows for confidential conversations and that screens are not visible to unauthorized persons (including family members).
*   **Equipment Security:** Organization-owned equipment must be password-protected and secured when not in use. Personal devices used for work (BYOD) must adhere to the *Mobile Device Security Config Standard*.
*   **Network Security:** Remote work must be conducted over a secure internet connection. Use of public Wi-Fi without the Organization’s approved VPN is prohibited.

#### 4.5. Dependent Care
Remote work is not a substitute for childcare or dependent care.
*   **Arrangements:** Employees must ensure that adequate care arrangements are in place for dependents during work hours.
*   **Flexibility:** While the Organization supports flexibility, the employee’s primary focus during work hours must be on job performance.

#### 4.6. Equipment and Expenses
*   **Organization Property:** Equipment provided by the Organization remains the property of Study GRC Inc. and must be returned immediately upon termination of this agreement or employment.
*   **Reimbursement:** The Organization will reimburse reasonable business expenses (e.g., portion of internet costs) only if pre-approved and in accordance with the *Remote Work Expense Reimbursement Policy* and *2 CFR 200* (for grant-funded roles).

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Employee / Volunteer** | Maintains a safe workspace; accurately tracks time (if non-exempt); protects Organization data; reports injuries immediately. |
| **Supervisor** | Defines work schedules; pre-approves overtime; monitors performance and deliverables; ensures regular communication. |
| **Human Resources** | Maintains signed agreements; processes Workers' Comp claims; advises on FLSA classifications. |
| **CISO** | Sets security standards for remote connectivity and equipment; audits compliance. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
Failure to comply with this agreement may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or volunteer assignment.
*   **Revocation:** The Organization may revoke remote work privileges (requiring a transition to a different arrangement or termination if the role cannot be performed elsewhere) for failure to meet performance goals or security requirements.
*   **Legal Action:** Theft or unauthorized retention of Organization equipment or data upon separation will be pursued legally.
