### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this Local Chapter Affiliation Agreement ("Agreement") is to legally define the relationship between **Study GRC Inc.** ("Parent Organization") and the local group identified in this Agreement ("Chapter"). This document establishes the terms under which the Chapter is granted a charter to operate using the Parent Organization’s name, tax-exempt status, and intellectual property to advance the mission defined in **Article I, Section 1.3** of the Bylaws.

### 2. Scope
This Agreement applies to the authorized leadership of the Chapter, its volunteers, and its operations. It governs the Chapter's use of the Parent Organization’s brand, financial resources, and legal status.
*   **Inclusions:** All activities, events, and communications conducted under the name "Study GRC [Location/Topic]."
*   **Exclusions:** Personal business activities of Chapter leaders not related to the Organization’s mission.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Parent Organization** | Study GRC Inc., a Texas Non-Profit Corporation and 501(c)(3) entity. |
| **Chapter** | The local unincorporated association or committee authorized by this Agreement to operate in a specific geographic area or subject domain. |
| **Charter** | The official authorization granted by the Parent Organization’s Board of Directors permitting the Chapter to operate. |
| **Good Standing** | The status of a Chapter that is current on all reporting requirements, financial disclosures, and adheres to all policies. |
| **Intellectual Property (IP)** | All trademarks, logos, curriculum, code, and content owned by Study GRC Inc. per **Bylaws Article IX**. |

### 4. Policy Statements & Agreement Terms

#### 4.1. Legal Relationship and Grant of Charter
4.1.1 **Nature of Relationship:** The Chapter is not a separate legal entity. It is an operating division (unincorporated association) of the Parent Organization. The Chapter has no authority to bind the Parent Organization to contracts, debts, or obligations without express written consent from the Executive Director.
4.1.2 **Grant of License:** Subject to the terms of this Agreement, the Parent Organization grants the Chapter a non-exclusive, revocable, royalty-free license to use the name "Study GRC," the logo, and associated branding solely for charitable and educational purposes within the Territory defined in **Appendix A**.

#### 4.2. Governance and Membership
4.2.1 **Adherence to Bylaws:** The Chapter agrees to abide by the **Bylaws of Study GRC Inc.**, specifically **Article II (Membership)**. The Chapter may not create separate classes of membership or charge dues that conflict with the Parent Organization's Membership Policy Manual.
4.2.2 **Leadership:** The Chapter must maintain, at a minimum, a Chapter President and a Chapter Secretary/Treasurer. These individuals must be **Supporting Members** in good standing as defined in **Bylaws Section 2.1**.
4.2.3 **Conflict of Interest:** All Chapter leaders must sign the **Annual Conflict of Interest Disclosure Statement** (GOV-FRM-002) and adhere to **Bylaws Article IX, Section 9.1**.

#### 4.3. Financial Management and Tax Status
4.3.1 **Tax-Exempt Status:** The Chapter operates under the Parent Organization’s 501(c)(3) group exemption (if applicable) or direct EIN. The Chapter must not engage in any political campaigning or substantial lobbying, strictly adhering to **Article I, Section 1.3** of the Bylaws.
4.3.2 **Bank Accounts:** The Chapter **may not** open a bank account in its own name or using the Parent Organization’s EIN without specific written resolution from the Parent Organization’s Board of Directors.
4.3.3 **Revenue and Expenses:** All funds raised by the Chapter are the property of Study GRC Inc.
    *   Donations must be processed through the Parent Organization’s central payment systems.
    *   Expenses will be reimbursed according to the **Travel and Expense Reimbursement Policy** (FIN-POL-037).
4.3.4 **Prohibition on Inurement:** No Chapter funds may inure to the benefit of any private individual, per **Bylaws Section 1.4**.

#### 4.4. Intellectual Property and Content
4.4.1 **Ownership:** Pursuant to **Bylaws Article IX, Section 9.3**, all content, curriculum, recordings, and materials created by the Chapter or its volunteers in service of the mission are "works made for hire" and are the sole property of Study GRC Inc.
4.4.2 **Open Source Commitment:** The Chapter acknowledges the Parent Organization’s commitment to open-source resources. The Chapter may not lock resources behind paywalls or claim exclusive copyright over community-generated content.

#### 4.5. Youth Protection and Safety
4.5.1 **Mandatory Compliance:** Recognizing the Organization’s K-12 focus, the Chapter must strictly adhere to the **Child and Youth Safeguarding Policy** (HR-POL-060).
4.5.2 **Background Checks:** Any Chapter leader or volunteer with unsupervised access to minors (virtual or physical) must undergo a background check per the **Background Check Policy** (HR-POL-024) prior to service.
4.5.3 **Two-Deep Leadership:** No Chapter volunteer may ever be alone with a minor. The "Two-Deep" rule applies to physical rooms and virtual breakout sessions.

#### 4.6. Reporting and Data Privacy
4.6.1 **Data Ownership:** All member data collected by the Chapter (email lists, registration data) is the property of Study GRC Inc. and must be managed in compliance with the **Master Data Privacy Policy** (IS-POL-001).
4.6.2 **Reporting:** The Chapter must submit a quarterly activity report and an annual financial summary to the Director of Kindness and Generosity (DKG).

#### 4.7. Term and Termination
4.7.1 **Term:** This Agreement is effective upon signature and renews automatically annually unless terminated.
4.7.2 **Termination for Cause:** The Parent Organization may revoke the Charter immediately for cause, including but not limited to: violation of the Bylaws, financial malfeasance, failure to implement youth protection safeguards, or conduct damaging to the Organization’s reputation.
4.7.3 **Effect of Termination:** Upon termination, the Chapter must immediately cease using the name "Study GRC," turn over all records and assets (digital and physical) to the Parent Organization, and dissolve any local administrative structures.

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Parent Board of Directors** | Approves the issuance and revocation of Chapter Charters. |
| **Executive Director** | Serves as the primary signatory for this Agreement; enforces operational compliance. |
| **Director of Kindness & Generosity (DKG)** | Primary point of contact for Chapter support; monitors Chapter health and reporting. |
| **Chapter President** | Signatory for the Chapter; ensures local compliance with this Agreement and Parent policies. |
| **Chapter Treasurer** | Ensures all local financial activities (if authorized) align with Parent financial policies. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
Failure to comply with this Agreement may result in:
1.  **Probation:** Temporary suspension of Chapter privileges and funding.
2.  **Revocation:** Permanent dissolution of the Chapter charter.
3.  **Legal Action:** Civil action for trademark infringement or recovery of misappropriated funds/assets.
