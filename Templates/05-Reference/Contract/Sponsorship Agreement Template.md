### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this Sponsorship Agreement ("Agreement") is to formalize the relationship between **Study GRC Inc.** ("Organization") and the **Sponsor**. This document ensures that all sponsorship activities align with the Organization’s 501(c)(3) charitable mission, comply with IRS regulations regarding "Qualified Sponsorship Payments" (IRC § 513(i)) to minimize Unrelated Business Income Tax (UBIT) exposure, and protect the Organization's reputation and intellectual property.

### 2. Scope
This Agreement applies to all corporate entities, organizations, or individuals ("Sponsors") providing financial support or in-kind contributions to Study GRC Inc. in exchange for public acknowledgment.

**Exclusions:**
*   **Federal Grants:** Governed by 2 CFR 200 and specific Notice of Award terms.
*   **Individual Philanthropy:** Pure donations with no return benefits (governed by the *Gift Acceptance Policy*).
*   **Vendor Contracts:** Procurement of goods/services (governed by the *Procurement Policy*).

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Qualified Sponsorship Payment** | A payment made by a sponsor where there is no arrangement or expectation that the sponsor will receive any substantial return benefit other than the use or acknowledgment of the name or logo of the sponsor. |
| **Advertising** | Any message or other programming material which promotes or markets any trade or business, or any service, facility, or product. Advertising includes messages containing qualitative or comparative language, price information, or other indications of savings or value, an endorsement, or an inducement to purchase, sell, or use any company, service, facility, or product. |
| **Acknowledgment** | Neutral recognition of the Sponsor's support, including logos and slogans that do not contain qualitative or comparative descriptions of the Sponsor's products or services. |
| **Sponsorship Benefits** | The specific list of acknowledgments (e.g., logo placement, social media mentions) agreed upon in Exhibit A. |

### 4. Agreement Terms

This Agreement is entered into on **[Date]** by and between **Study GRC Inc.**, a Texas Non-Profit Corporation ("Organization"), and **[Sponsor Name]**, a [State] [Entity Type] ("Sponsor").

#### 4.1. Engagement and Mission Alignment
The Sponsor agrees to provide financial or in-kind support to the Organization to further its mission of fostering a community-driven GRC ecosystem and providing zero-cost resources. The Sponsor acknowledges that the Organization is a tax-exempt entity under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code and that this relationship is intended to constitute a "Qualified Sponsorship Payment."

#### 4.2. Sponsorship Fee and Payment
*   **4.2.1 Fee:** Sponsor shall pay the Organization the sum of **$[Amount]** (the "Sponsorship Fee").
*   **4.2.2 Payment Schedule:** Payment is due [e.g., in full upon execution / Net 30 days from invoice date].
*   **4.2.3 In-Kind Contributions:** If applicable, defined in **Exhibit A**.

#### 4.3. Acknowledgement vs. Advertising (UBIT Compliance)
To ensure compliance with IRS regulations, the Organization will provide **Acknowledgment** of the Sponsor, not **Advertising**.
*   **4.3.1 Permissible Content:** The Organization may display the Sponsor’s logo, slogan (so long as it contains no qualitative or comparative descriptions), and website URL.
*   **4.3.2 Prohibited Content:** The Organization will **not** provide:
    *   Qualitative or comparative language (e.g., "The Best GRC Tool").
    *   Price information or indications of savings/value.
    *   Endorsements of the Sponsor’s products or services.
    *   Inducements to purchase, sell, or use (Call to Action, e.g., "Buy Now").
*   **4.3.3 Final Approval:** The Organization retains final approval authority over the placement, design, and content of all acknowledgments to ensure they do not constitute unrelated business income.

#### 4.4. Independence of Content and Curriculum
Pursuant to the Organization's commitment to open-source and unbiased education:
*   **4.4.1 No Influence:** The Sponsor shall have **no right** to direct, control, vet, or censor the Organization’s educational content, curriculum, community discussions, or research.
*   **4.4.2 No Paywalls:** Sponsorship shall not result in the gating or paywalling of any resource that is designated as open-source by the Organization.

#### 4.5. Intellectual Property (License)
*   **4.5.1 Sponsor IP:** Sponsor grants the Organization a non-exclusive, royalty-free, limited license to use the Sponsor’s name, logo, and trademarks solely for the purpose of fulfilling the Sponsorship Benefits defined in Exhibit A.
*   **4.5.2 Organization IP:** The Sponsor acknowledges that all content, code, and materials produced by Study GRC Inc. remain the sole property of the Organization (or are licensed under Open Source terms as applicable), pursuant to **Bylaws Article IX, Section 9.3**.

#### 4.6. Term and Termination
*   **4.6.1 Term:** This Agreement commences on [Start Date] and terminates on [End Date].
*   **4.6.2 Termination for Cause:** Either party may terminate this Agreement upon material breach by the other party, provided the breach is not cured within ten (10) days of written notice.
*   **4.6.3 Morals Clause (Reputational Risk):** The Organization may terminate this Agreement immediately, without refund, if the Sponsor or its senior leadership engages in conduct that:
    *   Violates the Organization’s *Child and Youth Safeguarding Policy*.
    *   Is convicted of a felony or crime involving fraud or dishonesty.
    *   Engages in public conduct that, in the Organization’s sole discretion, brings the Organization into disrepute or conflicts with its mission of ethical GRC practices.

#### 4.7. Indemnification and Liability
*   **4.7.1 Indemnification:** Sponsor agrees to indemnify and hold harmless Study GRC Inc., its Board, Officers, and volunteers from any claims, damages, or liabilities arising out of the Sponsor’s use of the Sponsorship Benefits or the Sponsor's products/services.
*   **4.7.2 Limitation of Liability:** The Organization’s liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total amount of the Sponsorship Fee actually paid by the Sponsor.

#### 4.8. Miscellaneous
*   **4.8.1 Governing Law:** This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Texas.
*   **4.8.2 Entire Agreement:** This document constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
Failure to adhere to the "Acknowledgment vs. Advertising" distinction (Section 4.3) may result in the reclassification of sponsorship revenue as Unrelated Business Taxable Income (UBTI), creating tax liability for the Organization.

**Enforcement:**
*   The Organization reserves the right to reject any Sponsor logo or material that violates the *Child and Youth Safeguarding Policy* or *Code of Ethics*.
*   Violation of the payment terms may result in the immediate suspension of Sponsorship Benefits.
