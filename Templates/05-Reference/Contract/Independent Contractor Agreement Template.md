### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this document is to establish a standardized legal framework for engaging Independent Contractors. This template ensures compliance with Internal Revenue Service (IRS) worker classification rules (Form 1099 reporting), protects the Intellectual Property rights of Study GRC Inc. as mandated by Bylaws Article IX, and defines the scope of liability and confidentiality for non-employee service providers.

### 2. Scope
This agreement applies to all individuals, sole proprietors, and business entities engaged by Study GRC Inc. ("the Organization") to perform specific services for a fee, who are **not** classified as W-2 employees.

*   **Applies to:** Consultants, freelance developers, curriculum writers, guest speakers receiving honoraria >$600, and third-party service providers.
*   **Exclusions:** W-2 Employees, Board Directors (serving in their governance capacity), and Volunteers receiving nominal reimbursement only.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Contractor** | The individual or entity providing services under this Agreement. |
| **Deliverables** | All documents, code, curriculum, designs, reports, and other tangible materials created by the Contractor for the Organization. |
| **Confidential Information** | Non-public information including member lists, donor data, unpublished curriculum, and security vulnerabilities. |
| **Work Made for Hire** | A legal term defined in the U.S. Copyright Act indicating that the employer (Organization) owns the copyright to the work created. |

### 4. Agreement Terms and Conditions
*Note: The following sections constitute the mandatory legal terms to be executed between the Organization and the Contractor.*

#### 4.1 Engagement and Services
The Organization hereby engages the Contractor, and the Contractor hereby accepts the engagement, to perform the services described in the attached **Statement of Work (SOW)** or **Exhibit A**. The Contractor shall determine the method, details, and means of performing the Services.

#### 4.2 Independent Contractor Status (IRS Compliance)
**4.2.1 No Employer-Employee Relationship:** It is the express intention of the parties that the Contractor is an independent contractor and not an employee, agent, joint venturer, or partner of the Organization.
**4.2.2 Control:** The Organization is interested only in the results to be achieved. The Contractor retains sole control over the manner and means of performing the services.
**4.2.3 Equipment and Expenses:** Unless otherwise specified in the SOW, the Contractor shall provide all equipment, tools, and supplies necessary to perform the services at their own expense.
**4.2.4 No Benefits:** The Contractor is not entitled to any employee benefits, including but not limited to health insurance, retirement plans, workers' compensation, or unemployment insurance.

#### 4.3 Compensation and Taxes
**4.3.1 Fees:** The Organization shall pay the Contractor the fee set forth in the SOW.
**4.3.2 No Withholding:** The Organization will **not** withhold any federal, state, or local income tax, Social Security, or Medicare tax from payments.
**4.3.3 Tax Reporting:** The Contractor is solely responsible for reporting and paying all applicable taxes. The Organization shall issue IRS Form 1099-NEC to the Contractor if total compensation exceeds $600 in a calendar year, pursuant to IRS regulations.
**4.3.4 W-9 Requirement:** Payment is contingent upon the Contractor providing a completed and signed IRS Form W-9.

#### 4.4 Intellectual Property (Bylaws Article IX Compliance)
**4.4.1 Work Made for Hire:** Pursuant to **Bylaws Article IX, Section 9.3**, the Contractor agrees that all Deliverables created during this engagement are "works made for hire." The Organization shall be the sole and exclusive owner of all rights, title, and interest in and to the Deliverables.
**4.4.2 Assignment:** To the extent any Deliverable does not qualify as a "work made for hire," the Contractor hereby irrevocably assigns to the Organization all right, title, and interest (including all intellectual property rights) in such Deliverables.
**4.4.3 Open Source Commitment:** The Contractor acknowledges the Organization’s mission to provide open-source resources and agrees that the Organization has the right to license the Deliverables under open-source licenses (e.g., Creative Commons, MIT) at its sole discretion.

#### 4.5 Confidentiality and Data Protection
**4.5.1 Non-Disclosure:** The Contractor agrees to hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence and not to disclose it to any third party without prior written consent, in accordance with **Bylaws Article IX, Section 9.2**.
**4.5.2 Data Privacy:** If the Contractor processes Personal Data (as defined by GDPR/TDPSA), they agree to comply with the Organization’s **Master Data Privacy Policy [GOV-POL-001]** and execute a separate Data Processing Agreement (DPA) if required.

#### 4.6 Youth Protection and Background Checks
**4.6.1 Requirement:** Due to the Organization’s K-12 focus, if the Services involve direct interaction with minors or access to data regarding minors, the Contractor must submit to a background check prior to commencing work.
**4.6.2 Disqualification:** Failure to pass the background check in accordance with the **Background Check Policy [HR-POL-005]** will result in immediate termination of this Agreement.

#### 4.7 Indemnification
The Contractor agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the Organization, its officers, directors, and agents from any and all claims, demands, liabilities, suits, costs, and expenses (including reasonable attorney’s fees) arising out of the Contractor’s negligence, willful misconduct, or breach of this Agreement.

#### 4.8 Termination
**4.8.1 At-Will:** Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time, with or without cause, upon [Number] days' written notice.
**4.8.2 Breach:** The Organization may terminate this Agreement immediately if the Contractor breaches any material term, including failure to deliver Services or violation of the Code of Conduct.

#### 4.9 Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Texas, without regard to its conflict of laws principles.

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Hiring Manager** | Define the Statement of Work (SOW), verify deliverables, and approve invoices. |
| **Contractor** | Perform services, pay own taxes, and maintain confidentiality. |
| **Chief Financial Officer** | Collect Form W-9, process payments, and file IRS Form 1099-NEC annually. |
| **HR/Compliance** | Ensure background checks are completed (if applicable) and retain the contract. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
Failure to utilize this template for independent contractor engagements may result in disciplinary action for the hiring manager.
**Contractor Violation:** Violation of the terms by the Contractor may result in immediate contract termination, withholding of payment, and legal action for damages or IP infringement.
