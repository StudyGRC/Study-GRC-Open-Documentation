### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this Standard is to mandate the preparation, content, and distribution of the Annual Financial Report for Study GRC Inc. (the "Organization"). This document ensures compliance with **Texas Business Organizations Code (TBOC) § 22.352**, which requires non-profit corporations to maintain financial records and prepare an annual report. Furthermore, this standard operationalizes the transparency commitments made in **Article VIII, Section 8.2** of the Bylaws, ensuring Voting Members and the public have access to the Organization’s financial health and stewardship of funds.

### 2. Scope
This standard applies to the Board of Directors, the Treasurer, the Chief Financial Officer (CFO), and any external accounting firms engaged by the Organization. It covers the financial activities of the Organization for the preceding fiscal year (January 1 – December 31).

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Statement of Financial Position** | Also known as a Balance Sheet; a report listing the Organization's assets, liabilities, and net assets at a specific point in time. |
| **Statement of Activities** | Also known as an Income Statement or P&L; a report showing revenue, expenses, and changes in net assets over a period of time. |
| **Functional Expenses** | The classification of expenses into program services, management/general, and fundraising categories. |
| **Net Assets** | The residual interest in the Organization's assets after deducting liabilities (equivalent to "Equity" in for-profit accounting). |
| **TBOC** | Texas Business Organizations Code. |
| **Voting Members** | Supporting Members and Honorary Members as defined in Bylaws Article II. |

### 4. Standards and Requirements

#### 4.1 Legal Mandate and Authority
Pursuant to **TBOC § 22.352** and **Bylaws Article VIII, Section 8.2**, the Organization must prepare a written financial report annually. This report is distinct from the IRS Form 990, though data may overlap.

#### 4.2 Reporting Period and Timeline
4.2.1 **Fiscal Year:** The report must cover the Organization’s fiscal year, which is the calendar year (January 1 through December 31), per **Bylaws Article VIII, Section 8.3**.
4.2.2 **Deadline:** The report must be prepared and approved by the Board of Directors prior to the Annual Meeting of Members held in January.
4.2.3 **Availability:** The report must be available to the public and members no later than January 31st of the year following the reporting period.

#### 4.3 Required Content
The Annual Financial Report must include, at a minimum, the following components regarding the corporation's transactions for the preceding year:

**4.3.1 Statement of Support, Revenue, and Expenses**
*   Must detail all sources of income (e.g., membership dues, donations, grants, program service revenue).
*   Must detail all expenses incurred.

**4.3.2 Statement of Changes in Fund Balances**
*   Must show the change in Net Assets (Beginning Balance vs. Ending Balance).
*   Must reconcile any adjustments or transfers between funds.

**4.3.3 Statement of Functional Expenses**
*   Expenses must be categorized by function:
    *   **Program Services:** Costs directly related to the mission (e.g., curriculum development, open-source hosting, community events).
    *   **Management and General:** Administrative costs (e.g., insurance, software licenses, bank fees).
    *   **Fundraising:** Costs incurred in soliciting contributions.

**4.3.4 Statement of Financial Position (Balance Sheet)**
*   Must list all Assets (Cash, Accounts Receivable, Equipment).
*   Must list all Liabilities (Accounts Payable, Deferred Revenue).
*   Must show Net Assets (Unrestricted, Temporarily Restricted, Permanently Restricted).

#### 4.4 Accounting Basis
The report must be prepared in accordance with Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP) or the Modified Cash Basis of accounting, consistent with the method used for the Organization's IRS Form 990 filing.

#### 4.5 Approval and Certification
4.5.1 The CFO or Treasurer must certify the accuracy of the report.
4.5.2 The Board of Directors must review and approve the report by a majority vote.
4.5.3 The report must be signed by the President and the Treasurer.

#### 4.6 Distribution and Transparency
4.6.1 **Member Access:** In accordance with **Bylaws Article II, Section 2.6**, the report must be made available to any Voting Member upon request.
4.6.2 **Public Posting:** To satisfy **Bylaws Article VIII, Section 8.2**, the approved Annual Financial Report must be published on the Organization’s designated "Transparency" webpage.
4.6.3 **Record Retention:** A physical or secure digital copy of the report must be kept in the Organization’s principal office for at least three (3) years, as required by **TBOC § 22.353(b)**.

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Chief Financial Officer (CFO)** | Prepares the preliminary financial statements; ensures reconciliation of bank accounts; drafts the report. |
| **Treasurer** | Reviews the draft report for accuracy and compliance; presents the report to the Board for approval; signs the final report. |
| **Board of Directors** | Reviews and approves the Annual Financial Report; ensures the report aligns with the Organization's mission and financial reality. |
| **President** | Signs the final report alongside the Treasurer. |
| **Secretary** | Ensures the approved report is archived in the corporate minute book and uploaded to the Transparency webpage. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
Failure to prepare and maintain this report constitutes a violation of Texas law and the Organization's Bylaws.
*   **Legal Consequence:** Under **TBOC § 22.354**, failure to prepare the report allows a member to bring an action against the corporation to force compliance, potentially resulting in the Organization being liable for the member's attorney fees and court costs.
*   **Internal Discipline:** Officers found negligent in the preparation of this report may be subject to removal from office under **Bylaws Article IV, Section 4.3** and **Article V, Section 5.3**.
