### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this Standard is to establish a centralized, mandatory **Contract Template Repository** for Study GRC Inc. This ensures that all legal agreements entered into by the Organization are consistent, legally sound, and aligned with risk management protocols. By utilizing pre-approved templates, the Organization minimizes liability, protects Intellectual Property in accordance with **Bylaws Article IX**, and ensures compliance with federal grant regulations (2 CFR 200).

### 2. Scope
This Standard applies to all Directors, Officers, employees, and volunteers authorized to initiate, negotiate, or execute agreements on behalf of Study GRC Inc.

*   **Inclusions:** All recurring legal agreements, including but not limited to NDAs, vendor contracts, volunteer waivers, speaker agreements, and employment offers.
*   **Exclusions:** Bespoke, high-value, or complex transactions (e.g., mergers, real estate acquisitions) which require direct drafting and review by Outside Counsel.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Repository** | The designated, secure digital location (e.g., Legal Sharepoint/Drive) where approved templates are stored. |
| **Master Template** | A locked document version containing standard legal terms approved by the General Counsel. |
| **Variable Fields** | Specific sections of a template (highlighted or bracketed) that may be modified by the user (e.g., names, dates, dollar amounts). |
| **Boilerplate** | Standardized legal language (e.g., indemnification, governing law) that must not be altered without approval. |
| **Redline** | A version of a document showing changes, deletions, and additions compared to the Master Template. |

### 4. Standards and Procedures

#### 4.1 Repository Location and Access
4.1.1 The **Contract Template Repository** shall be maintained within the Organization’s secure document management system under the folder directory: `00_Central_Operations > Legal > 01_Templates`.
4.1.2 Read-only access is granted to all Department Heads and Operational Officers.
4.1.3 Write/Edit access to the Repository is restricted strictly to the Chief Legal Officer (CLO) and the Executive Director.

#### 4.2 Mandatory Use Requirement
4.2.1 Personnel initiating a new relationship or transaction **must** utilize the most current version of the appropriate Master Template from the Repository.
4.2.2 The use of outdated versions saved on local hard drives or personal devices is strictly prohibited.
4.2.3 If a specific template does not exist for a proposed transaction, the initiating party must submit a request to the Legal Department to draft a new agreement or approve a third-party paper.

#### 4.3 Approved Template Categories
The Repository is organized into the following sub-directories. All templates must contain the specific clauses mandated by **Bylaws Article IX (IP)** and **Article VII (Indemnification)**.

**A. General Corporate & Governance**
*   `LGL-TMP-001` **Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA):** For partnerships/discussions.
*   `LGL-TMP-002` **Unilateral NDA:** For vendors accessing Org data.
*   `LGL-TMP-003` **Conflict of Interest Disclosure Form:** Annual Board/Officer requirement.

**B. Human Resources & Volunteers**
*   `HR-TMP-001` **Offer of Employment Letter:** At-will employment terms.
*   `HR-TMP-002` **Independent Contractor Agreement (ICA):** Includes "Work Made for Hire" assignment.
*   `HR-TMP-003` **Volunteer Service Agreement & Waiver:** Liability release for general volunteers.
*   `HR-TMP-004` **Employee Handbook Acknowledgment:** Receipt of policies.

**C. Programs & Events**
*   `OPS-TMP-001` **Speaker/Presenter Agreement:** IP release for recording/distributing content.
*   `OPS-TMP-002` **Sponsorship Agreement:** Defines benefits and UBIT compliance.
*   `OPS-TMP-003` **Event Liability Waiver (Participant):** Standard release for attendees.
*   `OPS-TMP-004` **Parental Consent Form:** For participants under 18 (COPPA/Safety compliance).

**D. Procurement (Federal Grant Compliant)**
*   `FIN-TMP-001` **Master Service Agreement (MSA):** For long-term vendors.
*   `FIN-TMP-002` **Statement of Work (SOW):** Task-specific annex to MSA.
*   `FIN-TMP-003` **Federal Vendor Rider:** Mandatory 2 CFR 200 Appendix II clauses (Debarment, Anti-Lobbying, etc.).

#### 4.4 Modification and Negotiation
4.4.1 **Permissible Changes:** Users may populate Variable Fields (names, dates, scope, costs) without legal review.
4.4.2 **Prohibited Changes:** Users may **not** alter Boilerplate clauses (Indemnification, Limitation of Liability, Governing Law, IP Ownership) without express written approval from the CLO.
4.4.3 **Third-Party Redlines:** If a counterparty returns a template with redlines/edits:
*   Minor administrative changes may be accepted by the Department Head.
*   Substantive legal changes must be escalated to the Legal Department for review.

#### 4.5 Version Control and Maintenance
4.5.1 All templates must follow the naming convention: `[ID]_[Name]_v[YYYY.MM]`.
*   *Example:* `HR-TMP-002_ContractorAgreement_v2023.10`
4.5.2 The Legal Department shall conduct a **mandatory annual review** of all templates to ensure compliance with changing laws (e.g., Texas Business Organizations Code, Federal Grant Regulations).
4.5.3 Archived/Retired templates must be moved to the `Z_Archive` folder to prevent accidental use.

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Chief Legal Officer** | Creates, updates, and approves all Master Templates. Owns the Repository structure. |
| **Executive Director** | Enforces the use of templates across the organization. Approves deviations from standard terms when business risk is acceptable. |
| **Department Heads** | Ensure staff use correct templates. Populate Variable Fields accurately. |
| **All Staff/Volunteers** | Download fresh templates for every new transaction. Do not reuse old files. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
Failure to utilize approved templates or unauthorized modification of legal terms puts the Organization at significant risk.
*   Contracts signed without using the approved template or without required Legal review may be deemed **voidable** internally.
*   Employees or Officers who repeatedly execute non-compliant agreements may face disciplinary action, up to and including termination, and personal liability for unauthorized commitments as per **Bylaws Article VII**.
