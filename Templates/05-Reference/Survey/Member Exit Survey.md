### 1. Purpose
The purpose of the **Member Exit Survey** is to gather structured feedback from departing Supporting Members and Volunteers to identify the root causes of membership churn. This instrument serves as a critical mechanism for **Continuous Improvement**, allowing Study GRC Inc. to refine its value proposition, detect potential grievances or ethical violations, and improve retention strategies.

### 2. Scope
This standard applies to:
*   **Supporting Members** (Voting) who voluntarily resign or allow their membership to lapse.
*   **Volunteers** who formally resign from their service commitments.

**Exclusions:**
*   This process does not apply to members or volunteers who are expelled or removed for cause (e.g., violations of the Code of Conduct), as those separations are handled via the *Member Expulsion Due Process Policy*.
*   Community Participants (Non-Voting) who simply cease activity in public forums.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Churn** | The rate at which members or subscribers stop paying or participating over a given period. |
| **Lapsed Member** | A Supporting Member whose dues are in arrears for more than 30 days and has not renewed. |
| **Red Flag Response** | A survey response indicating legal, ethical, or safety concerns (e.g., harassment, fraud, discrimination) requiring immediate escalation. |
| **NPS (Net Promoter Score)** | A metric used to measure member loyalty and satisfaction based on the likelihood of recommending the organization to others. |

### 4. Procedures and Survey Standard

#### 4.1 Triggering Events
The Member Exit Survey must be deployed under the following conditions:
1.  **Voluntary Resignation:** Within 48 hours of receiving written notice of resignation (per **Bylaws Article III, Section 3.7** for Directors, or general membership procedures).
2.  **Lapse of Dues:** 30 days after a Supporting Member’s subscription has expired without renewal.

#### 4.2 Administration Protocol
1.  **Distribution:** The survey is distributed electronically via the Organization’s secure CRM or survey platform.
2.  **Anonymity:** Members must be given the option to submit responses anonymously, except where specific follow-up is requested by the member.
3.  **Data Retention:** Survey data is retained in accordance with the *Data Retention and Destruction Policy* and *Privacy Policy*.

#### 4.3 Escalation of "Red Flag" Responses
If a respondent indicates that their departure is due to harassment, discrimination, financial impropriety, or safety concerns:
1.  The **Director of Kindness and Generosity (DKG)** must immediately flag the response.
2.  The response must be forwarded to the **Board Secretary** and/or **Legal Counsel** within 24 hours.
3.  The *Whistleblower Investigation Procedure* or *Sexual Harassment Investigation Procedure* is triggered automatically, regardless of whether the member formally filed a grievance.

#### 4.4 Standard Survey Questions
The following questions constitute the mandatory standard for the Exit Survey. The DKG may add questions but may not remove the core set without Board approval.

**Section A: Reason for Departure**
*Please select the primary reason for ending your membership/service:*
*   [ ] Financial constraints / Cost of dues.
*   [ ] Lack of time / Change in personal circumstances.
*   [ ] Lack of perceived value / Resources did not meet expectations.
*   [ ] Disagreement with organizational direction/mission.
*   [ ] Interpersonal conflict or community culture issues.
*   [ ] Technical issues (website/platform usability).
*   [ ] Other (Please specify).

**Section B: Satisfaction & Value (Likert Scale 1-5)**
*   *How would you rate the quality of the educational resources provided?*
*   *How would you rate the inclusivity and culture of the community?*
*   *How likely are you to recommend Study GRC Inc. to a colleague? (NPS)*

**Section C: Safety and Ethics (Mandatory)**
*   *Did you experience or witness any harassment, discrimination, or unethical behavior during your time with the Organization?*
    *   [ ] No.
    *   [ ] Yes (If yes, a text box appears: "Would you like to be contacted by a Board Officer regarding this matter?").

**Section D: Future Engagement**
*   *Would you consider rejoining Study GRC Inc. in the future?*
*   *What one change would have convinced you to stay?*

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Director of Kindness and Generosity (DKG)** | Owns the exit survey process; analyzes aggregate data; reports quarterly churn trends to the Board. |
| **Membership Coordinator** | Automates the distribution of the survey via the CRM upon membership lapse or resignation. |
| **Board Secretary** | Receives escalated "Red Flag" responses and initiates governance/compliance reviews. |
| **CISO** | Ensures the survey platform complies with *Data Privacy* and *GDPR* standards (specifically regarding the Right to Erasure if requested). |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
Failure to administer this survey results in a loss of critical strategic data and hinders the Organization's ability to meet its *Continuous Improvement* regulatory obligations.

**Critical Compliance Warning:** Failure to escalate a "Red Flag" response (allegations of abuse or fraud) constitutes a violation of the *Whistleblower Protection Policy* and may expose the Organization and individual officers to significant legal liability.
