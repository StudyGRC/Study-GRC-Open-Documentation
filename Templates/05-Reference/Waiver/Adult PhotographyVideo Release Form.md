### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this form is to secure the necessary legal permissions for Study GRC Inc. (the "Organization") to capture, record, and distribute the name, image, voice, and likeness of adult participants. This waiver mitigates legal risks associated with "Right of Publicity" claims (under Texas Property Code Chapter 26 and common law) and ensures the Organization can freely fulfill its educational mission by distributing open-source content.

### 2. Scope
*   **Applies to:** All adult individuals (18 years of age or older) participating in Organization activities, including but not limited to guest speakers, volunteers, event attendees, and interview subjects.
*   **Contexts:** Physical events, virtual meetings (e.g., Zoom, Discord stages), recorded webinars, and marketing photography.
*   **Exclusions:** This form is **not** valid for minors. Individuals under 18 must have a parent or guardian execute the *Parental Consent and Media Release Form (YTH-FRM-002)*.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Likeness** | The visual or auditory representation of an individual, including their physical appearance, voice, name, and biographical information. |
| **Media** | Any format of communication or recording, including but not limited to digital video, audio recordings, still photography, social media posts, website content, and print materials. |
| **Releasor** | The individual signing this form and granting rights to the Organization. |
| **Work Product** | As defined in Bylaws Article IX, content created for the Organization. While this form covers likeness, specific IP assignment for created materials is governed by the *Intellectual Property Policy*. |

### 4. Waiver and Release Statements

By signing below, the Releasor agrees to the following terms:

#### 4.1 Grant of Rights
I hereby grant Study GRC Inc., its Board of Directors, officers, employees, and agents (collectively, the "Releasees") a perpetual, irrevocable, worldwide, royalty-free, and non-exclusive license to use, reproduce, distribute, display, and create derivative works from my Likeness captured during Organization activities.

#### 4.2 Permitted Uses
I understand that my Likeness may be used for:
*   **Educational Resources:** Publication in the Organization’s open-source knowledge base, training videos, and course materials.
*   **Promotional Materials:** Use in newsletters, annual reports, social media, and the Organization's website to advance its charitable mission.
*   **Fundraising:** Materials used to demonstrate the Organization's impact to donors and grantors.

#### 4.3 Waiver of Inspection and Compensation
*   **No Compensation:** I acknowledge that I am participating voluntarily and will receive no financial compensation for the use of my Likeness, unless explicitly agreed upon in a separate *Speaker Agreement* or *Service Contract*.
*   **No Inspection:** I waive any right to inspect or approve the finished product, including written copy or video editing, wherein my Likeness appears.

#### 4.4 Ownership of Media
In accordance with **Bylaws Article IX (Intellectual Property)**, I acknowledge that Study GRC Inc. is the sole owner of the copyright in the photographs, video recordings, and audio recordings taken by the Organization or its agents, regardless of who appears in them.

#### 4.5 Release of Liability
To the fullest extent permitted by Texas law, I hereby release, discharge, and hold harmless the Releasees from any and all claims, demands, or causes of action that I have or may have in the future for libel, defamation, invasion of privacy, or right of publicity arising out of the use of my Likeness.

#### 4.6 Virtual Participation Acknowledgement
I acknowledge that Study GRC Inc. operates as a remote-first entity. By turning on my camera or microphone during a recorded virtual session, I consent to the recording and distribution of my feed as part of the session content.

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Releasor (Signer)** | Reviews the waiver, asks questions if necessary, and provides a signature prior to being recorded. |
| **Event Host / DKG** | Ensures all speakers and featured participants have signed this form prior to the start of the event. |
| **Marketing Team** | Verifies that a valid release is on file before featuring a specific individual in high-profile marketing campaigns. |
| **Secretary** | Maintains signed forms in accordance with the *Document Retention and Destruction Policy*. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
*   **Mandatory Requirement:** Failure to execute this release may result in the individual being prohibited from speaking roles, appearing on camera, or participating in recorded segments of events.
*   **Revocation:** Due to the open-source nature of the Organization's distribution channels, this release is irrevocable once content has been published. Requests to remove content will be handled at the sole discretion of the Board of Directors, based on technical feasibility and the *Data Privacy Policy*.
