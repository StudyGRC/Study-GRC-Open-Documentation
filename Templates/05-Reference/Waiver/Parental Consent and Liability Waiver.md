### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this document is to establish informed consent and a legal release of liability for minors participating in activities organized by Study GRC Inc. ("the Organization"). This waiver ensures that parents/legal guardians are fully aware of the nature of the Organization’s programs (both virtual and physical), the inherent risks involved, and the behavioral expectations required for participation. It serves to mitigate legal risk and protect the Organization’s assets and volunteers in accordance with Texas law.

### 2. Scope
This document applies to:
*   **Participants:** All individuals under the age of eighteen (18) seeking to participate in Study GRC Inc. programs, events, workshops, or online communities.
*   **Signatories:** Parents or Court-Appointed Legal Guardians of the Participant.
*   **Activities:** All remote/virtual sessions, physical meetups, hackathons, training courses, and community interactions managed by the Organization.

**Exclusions:** This waiver does not apply to adult participants (18+), who must sign the standard *Adult Participant Liability Waiver*.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Participant** | The minor child (under 18 years of age) being registered for the Activity. |
| **Guardian** | The parent or court-appointed legal guardian with the legal authority to enter into contracts on behalf of the Participant. |
| **Activity** | Any educational program, event, webinar, online forum, or gathering organized by Study GRC Inc. |
| **Released Parties** | Study GRC Inc., its Board of Directors, Officers, employees, volunteers, agents, sponsors, and facility providers. |
| **Virtual Environment** | Online platforms used for program delivery, including but not limited to Zoom, Discord, Slack, and Learning Management Systems (LMS). |

### 4. Agreement Terms and Conditions

By signing this document, the Guardian agrees to the following terms on behalf of themselves and the Participant:

#### 4.1 Permission to Participate
I, the undersigned Guardian, hereby grant permission for the Participant to attend and participate in Activities sponsored by Study GRC Inc. I acknowledge that these Activities may be conducted remotely (online) or in-person.

#### 4.2 Assumption of Risk (Conspicuous Notice)
**I UNDERSTAND THAT PARTICIPATION IN THE ACTIVITY INVOLVES INHERENT RISKS, SOME OF WHICH CANNOT BE ELIMINATED REGARDLESS OF THE CARE TAKEN TO AVOID INJURIES.**

*   **Virtual Risks:** I acknowledge that online participation involves risks including, but not limited to, exposure to unmoderated content from third parties, cyberbullying, data privacy breaches, and the physiological effects of prolonged screen time. While Study GRC Inc. implements strict moderation and security protocols (per the *Child and Youth Safeguarding Policy*), the Organization cannot guarantee total immunity from online threats.
*   **Physical Risks:** If the Activity is in-person, risks include minor injuries (scratches, bruises), major injuries, or catastrophic injuries.
*   **Voluntary Participation:** I acknowledge that the Participant’s involvement is voluntary and that I knowingly assume all such risks, both known and unknown.

#### 4.3 Release and Waiver of Liability
**TO THE FULLEST EXTENT PERMITTED BY TEXAS LAW, I HEREBY RELEASE, WAIVE, DISCHARGE, AND COVENANT NOT TO SUE THE RELEASED PARTIES FROM ANY AND ALL LIABILITY, CLAIMS, DEMANDS, ACTIONS, AND CAUSES OF ACTION WHATSOEVER ARISING OUT OF OR RELATED TO ANY LOSS, DAMAGE, OR INJURY, INCLUDING DEATH, THAT MAY BE SUSTAINED BY THE PARTICIPANT, OR TO ANY PROPERTY BELONGING TO ME OR THE PARTICIPANT, WHETHER CAUSED BY THE NEGLIGENCE OF THE RELEASED PARTIES OR OTHERWISE.**

#### 4.4 Indemnification
I agree to **INDEMNIFY AND HOLD HARMLESS** the Released Parties from any and all costs, expenses, and liabilities (including attorney’s fees) arising out of any claim or legal action brought by or on behalf of the Participant, or resulting from the Participant’s willful misconduct or breach of the *Youth Code of Conduct*. This aligns with the indemnification principles set forth in **Article VII** of the Organization’s Bylaws.

#### 4.5 Medical Consent (Emergency Authorization)
In the event of an emergency during an in-person Activity where I cannot be reached, I hereby authorize Study GRC Inc. staff or volunteers to secure medical treatment for the Participant. I agree to be financially responsible for any costs incurred for such treatment (ambulance, hospital, physician fees).

#### 4.6 Code of Conduct and Dismissal
I have reviewed the *Youth Code of Conduct* with the Participant. I understand that Study GRC Inc. maintains a zero-tolerance policy for bullying, harassment, or unauthorized use of technology. I agree that the Organization reserves the right to dismiss the Participant from the Activity without refund if their behavior is deemed disruptive or dangerous.

#### 4.7 Intellectual Property and Media Release
*   **Media Release:** I grant Study GRC Inc. the right to use the Participant’s image, likeness, and voice in photographs, video recordings, and electronic reproductions for educational and promotional purposes, without compensation.
*   **Work Product:** Pursuant to **Article IX, Section 9.3** of the Bylaws, I acknowledge that any collaborative educational materials or code created by the Participant during the Activity for the benefit of the open-source community shall be licensed under the Organization's standard Open Source License, ensuring the resources remain free and accessible.

#### 4.8 Digital Privacy and COPPA
I acknowledge that I have received and reviewed the *Member Data Privacy Notice*. I understand that for Participants under 13, Study GRC Inc. requires Verifiable Parental Consent pursuant to the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) before collecting any personal information. My signature below constitutes this consent.

#### 4.9 Severability and Venue
This agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Texas. If any portion of this agreement is held invalid, the remainder shall continue in full legal force and effect. Any legal proceedings related to this waiver shall take place in the county of the Organization’s Registered Office.

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Guardian** | Read, understand, and sign the waiver; explain risks and rules to the Participant; update emergency contact info. |
| **Participant** | Adhere to the *Youth Code of Conduct* and follow instructions from Organization leadership. |
| **Program Manager** | Verify that a completed, signed waiver is on file **before** admitting the Participant to any Activity (virtual or physical). |
| **Record Keeper** | Retain signed waivers in a secure, encrypted repository for the duration required by the *Records Retention Schedule* (typically Age of Majority + Statute of Limitations). |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
**Mandatory Requirement:** No minor shall be permitted to participate in any Study GRC Inc. Activity without this signed form on file.
*   **Failure to Comply:** Failure to provide a signed waiver will result in the immediate denial of access to the Activity.
*   **Fraudulent Signatures:** If it is discovered that a minor signed this form forging a Guardian's signature, the minor will be immediately expelled from the program, and the Guardian will be notified.
