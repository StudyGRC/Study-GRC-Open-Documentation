### 1. Purpose
The purpose of this **Bylaws Amendment History Log** is to maintain an immutable, chronological audit trail of all modifications, revisions, and restatements made to the Bylaws of Study GRC Inc. This document ensures compliance with **Texas Business Organizations Code (TBOC) § 22.353** regarding the maintenance of corporate records and supports accurate reporting on **IRS Form 990, Part VI, Section A**, regarding significant changes to the Organization’s governing documents.

### 2. Scope
This register applies to the **Bylaws of Study GRC Inc.** exclusively. It tracks:
*   Textual amendments approved by the Board of Directors.
*   Fundamental changes approved by the Voting Membership.
*   Restatements of the Bylaws.
*   Correction of clerical errors formally adopted by the Board.

This log does not track changes to lower-level policies, standards, or procedures, which are maintained in the *Master Policy Index (GOV-REG-001)*.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Amendment** | A formal change, addition, or deletion to the text of the Bylaws. |
| **Restated Bylaws** | A document that consolidates the original Bylaws and all subsequent amendments into a single document, superseding the previous version. |
| **Fundamental Action** | As defined in **Article X** of the Bylaws, actions requiring a two-thirds (2/3) vote of the Membership (e.g., changes to voting rights, dissolution, or merger). |
| **Protected Section** | As defined in **Article XII** of the Bylaws, sections regarding Member voting rights, Director removal, or Organization Purpose that require Membership approval to amend. |

### 4. Amendment Log Maintenance Procedures

#### 4.1 Custody and Maintenance
The **Corporate Secretary** is the designated custodian of this log. The log must be updated within **five (5) business days** following the formal adoption of any amendment.

#### 4.2 Recording Requirements
For every amendment, the following data points must be recorded in the Master Registry (Section 4.4):
1.  **Effective Date:** The date the amendment was adopted or became effective.
2.  **Article(s) Affected:** Specific citation of the Articles or Sections changed.
3.  **Approving Body:** Indication of whether the amendment was approved by the **Board of Directors** (Standard) or the **Voting Membership** (Protected Sections/Fundamental Actions).
4.  **Vote Tally:** The specific vote count (e.g., "5-0 Unanimous" or "Member Vote: 75% Affirmative").
5.  **Resolution ID:** Reference to the Board Resolution or Meeting Minutes ID where the vote is recorded.
6.  **Summary of Change:** A brief description of the modification.

#### 4.3 Version Control
Each amendment triggers an increment in the Bylaws version number:
*   **Major Version (e.g., v2.0):** Used for Restated Bylaws or significant structural overhauls.
*   **Minor Version (e.g., v1.1):** Used for specific Article amendments or clause adjustments.

### 5. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Corporate Secretary** | Maintains this log; certifies the accuracy of the recorded amendments; ensures the current Bylaws are published to the Transparency Page. |
| **Board of Directors** | Reviews and approves amendments in accordance with **Article XII**; ensures amendments do not conflict with the Certificate of Formation. |
| **Voting Members** | Vote on amendments affecting Protected Sections (Voting Rights, Director Removal, Purpose) per **Article XII, Section 12.1**. |
| **Legal Counsel** | Reviews proposed amendments for compliance with TBOC and IRS 501(c)(3) regulations prior to adoption. |

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement
Failure to maintain an accurate history of Bylaws amendments may result in:
*   **Loss of Corporate Veil:** Inability to prove valid corporate governance during litigation.
*   **Audit Findings:** Non-compliance with federal grant requirements (2 CFR 200) regarding organizational standing.
*   **IRS Penalties:** Inaccurate reporting on Form 990 regarding changes to governing documents.

Any Director or Officer found falsifying this log is subject to immediate removal for cause under **Article III, Section 3.6** of the Bylaws.
