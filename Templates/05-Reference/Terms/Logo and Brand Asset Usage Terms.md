### 1. Purpose
The purpose of these Terms is to protect the intellectual property, reputation, and distinct identity of Study GRC Inc. (the "Organization"). While the Organization is dedicated to open-source knowledge and community sharing, the Organization’s name, logos, and brand assets are proprietary identifiers that signify the source and quality of our resources. These Terms define the permitted and prohibited uses of these assets to prevent confusion, dilution, or misuse in the marketplace, ensuring compliance with the Lanham Act and Texas Business & Commerce Code.

### 2. Scope
These Terms apply to all individuals and entities seeking to use Study GRC Inc. Brand Assets, including but not limited to:
*   **Internal:** Employees, Directors, Officers, and Volunteers.
*   **External:** Voting Members (Supporting/Honorary), Community Participants, Local Chapters, Partners, Vendors, and the general public.

**Exclusions:** This document does not grant a license for Commercial Use. Commercial Use requires a separate, written Trademark Licensing Agreement executed by the Board of Directors.

### 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Brand Assets** | Collectively, the Organization’s name, logos, wordmarks, slogans, color palettes, typefaces, and other visual identity elements. |
| **The Marks** | Specifically, the name "Study GRC," "Study GRC Inc.," the Study GRC logo (symbol and logotype), and any localized Chapter logos. |
| **Commercial Use** | Use of the Marks on goods for sale, in paid advertising for third-party services, or in any manner that implies a paid endorsement or partnership where none exists. |
| **Nominative Fair Use** | The legal right to use a trademark to refer to the trademark owner or its goods/services for purposes of reporting, commentary, or identification (e.g., "I am a member of Study GRC"). |
| **Clear Space** | The mandatory empty space surrounding the logo to ensure legibility and visual distinction. |

### 4. Terms of Use

#### 4.1 Ownership and Rights
Pursuant to **Article IX, Section 9.3** of the Bylaws, the Organization retains all rights, title, and interest in and to the Brand Assets. Use of the Brand Assets does not grant the user any ownership rights. All goodwill generated by the use of the Marks inures to the benefit of Study GRC Inc.

#### 4.2 Permitted Uses (No Prior Approval Required)
The following uses are permitted without specific written consent, provided the user complies with the Visual Standards in Section 4.4:
1.  **Membership Identification:** Voting Members in good standing may use the "Member of Study GRC" badge on personal websites, LinkedIn profiles, or resumes.
2.  **Educational Attribution:** Using the logo to attribute the source of open-source materials derived from Study GRC, provided the attribution does not imply the Organization has reviewed or endorsed the derivative work.
3.  **Media and Press:** News organizations may use the Marks for editorial purposes when reporting on the Organization.
4.  **Linking:** Sites may use the logo as a visual link to `studygrc.org`.

#### 4.3 Prohibited Uses
The following actions are strictly prohibited:
1.  **Alteration:** Users must not modify the Marks in any way, including changing colors, stretching/distorting aspect ratios, rearranging elements, or adding effects (shadows, glows).
2.  **Confusing Similarity:** Users must not use the Marks in a way that suggests their product, service, or website is created by, sponsored by, or affiliated with Study GRC Inc. unless explicitly authorized.
3.  **Combination:** The Marks may not be combined with any other logos, trademarks, or names to create a new mark.
4.  **Merchandise:** Users may not print the Marks on physical goods (t-shirts, mugs, stickers) for sale or distribution without a licensing agreement.
5.  **Disparagement:** The Marks may not be used in connection with illegal activities, hate speech, or content that violates the Organization’s **Code of Ethics and Conduct**.
6.  **Domain Names:** Users may not register domain names containing "Study GRC" or confusingly similar variations.

#### 4.4 Visual Standards
To ensure consistency, all usage must adhere to the following:
*   **Official Files:** Only use high-resolution files provided on the Organization’s "Transparency" or "Media Kit" page. Do not screen-grab logos from the website.
*   **Clear Space:** The logo must be surrounded by clear space equal to at least 50% of the height of the icon.
*   **Backgrounds:** The logo should be placed on a background that provides sufficient contrast for legibility.
*   **Minimum Size:** The logo must not be reproduced at a size smaller than 24px in height (digital) or 0.25 inches (print).

#### 4.5 Local Chapter Usage
Local Chapters recognized under the **Local Chapter Affiliation Agreement** must:
1.  Use the specific "Chapter Logo" lockup provided by the Organization (e.g., "Study GRC | Austin").
2.  Not create their own distinct logos or mascots that replace the official Brand Assets.
3.  Adhere to the **Chapter Comms/Social Media Guidelines**.

### 6. Compliance and Enforcement

#### 6.1 Revocation of Rights
Study GRC Inc. reserves the right to revoke permission to use the Brand Assets at any time, for any reason, with or without cause. Upon notice of revocation, the user must immediately cease all use of the Marks.

#### 6.2 Violation Consequences
*   **Non-Commercial Violations:** Will typically result in a "Notice to Correct." Failure to comply may result in termination of Membership (per **Bylaws Article II**) or banning from community platforms.
*   **Commercial/Malicious Violations:** May result in immediate legal action, including Cease and Desist orders and pursuit of damages under the Lanham Act and applicable Texas state laws.

#### 6.3 Reporting Misuse
Suspected misuse of the Brand Assets should be reported immediately to the Board of Directors or the Executive Director via the official contact channels listed on the Organization's website.
